14:17: Then the king of Sodom went out to meet him at the Valley of Shaveh (that is, the King’s Valley), after his return from the slaughter of Chedorlaomer and the kings with him. 
14:18: Now Melchizedek the king of Salem brought out bread and wine; he was the priest of God Most High. 
14:19: He blessed Abram and said, “Blessed be Abram of God Most High, Creator of heaven and earth; 
14:20: and blessed be God Most High, Who delivered your enemies into your hands.” Then Abram gave him a tithe of all.
