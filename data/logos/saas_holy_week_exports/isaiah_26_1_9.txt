26:1: In that day they shall sing this song in the land of Judah: “Behold, we have a strong city; He will make our salvation its surrounding wall. 
26:2: Open the gates; let the people enter who keep righteousness and guard the truth, 
26:3: and who lay hold of the truth and keep peace, 
26:4: because they hope forever in You, O Lord, the great and eternal God, 
26:5: who humbles and brings down those who dwell on high; the strong cities You will cast down; You will bring them down to the ground. 
26:6: The feet of the gentle and the humble shall tread them underfoot. 
26:7: The way of the godly is straight; and the way of the godly is prepared. 
26:8: For the way of the Lord is judgment. We hope in Your name and in the remembrance of You, 
26:9: which our soul desires at night. My spirit rises early in the morning to You, O God, for Your commands are a light upon the earth. Learn righteousness, you who dwell on the earth.
