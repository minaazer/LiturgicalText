4:1: Hear the word of the Lord, You children of Israel, For the Lord has a judgment for the inhabitants of the land: “There is no truth or mercy Or know ledge of God in the land. 
4:2: Cursing and lying, murder and theft And adultery gushed forth in the land, And blood is mingled with blood. 
4:3: Therefore the land shall mourn And be diminished with all the things that dwell in it: The wild beasts of the field, the reptiles of the earth, And the birds of the sky, And the fish of the sea shall come to an end; 
4:4: That neither may any one judge, nor analyze another; But my people are as a priest who is spoken against. 
4:5: “Thus you shall be weak by day, And the prophet shall be weak with you; I have compared your mother to the night. 
4:6: My people are like as those who have no know ledge. Because you have rejected knowledge, I will also reject you from being a priest to Me; And because you have forgotten the law of your God, I also will forget your children. 
4:7: According to their multitude, So they sinned against Me; I will turn their glory into dishonor. 
4:8: They will eat the sins of My people And take away their lives because of their wrongdoings.
