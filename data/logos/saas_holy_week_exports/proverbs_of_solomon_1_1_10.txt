40:1: “Comfort, yes, comfort My people,” says God. 
40:2: “O priests, speak to the heart of Jerusalem, comfort her, for her humiliation is ended, her sin is pardoned; for she received from the Lord’s hand double for her sins.” 
40:3: The voice of one crying in the wilderness: “Prepare the way of the Lord; make straight the paths of our God. 
40:4: Every valley shall be filled, and every mountain and hill humbled; the crooked places shall be made straight, and the rough places into plains. 
40:5: The glory of the Lord shall be revealed, and all flesh shall see the salvation of God; for the Lord has spoken.”
