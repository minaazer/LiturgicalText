26:9: which our soul desires at night. My spirit rises early in the morning to You, O God, for Your commands are a light upon the earth. Learn righteousness, you who dwell on the earth. 
26:10: The ungodly man ceases; he will not learn righteousness on the earth; he will not do the truth; let the ungodly man be taken away, that he may not see the glory of the Lord. 
26:11: O Lord, Your arm is exalted, but they did not know it. But when they know it, they shall be ashamed, for jealousy will seize an untaught people; and now fire will devour the adversaries. 
26:12: O Lord our God, grant us peace, for You render everything to us. 
26:13: O Lord our God, possess us; O Lord, we know no other besides You; we name Your name. 
26:14: The dead will not see life; neither will physicians raise them. Therefore You brought evils upon them and destroyed them, and took away every male of theirs. 
26:15: Bring more evils on them, O Lord, on the glorious of the earth. 
26:16: O Lord, I remembered You in my hard circumstances. Your chastening to us was a small affliction. 
26:17: As a woman with child is in pain and cries out in her pangs, when she draws near the time of her delivery, so we became Your beloved because of Your fear, O Lord. 
26:18: We have been with child, we have been in pain, and we have given birth. We brought forth the spirit of Your salvation on the earth. But the inhabitants of the world shall fall. 
26:19: The dead shall rise up; and those in the tombs shall arise. Those in the earth shall be glad, for your dew is a healing for them, but the land of the ungodly shall come to an end.” 
26:20: Come, my people, enter your closets and shut your door; hide yourself for a very short while, until the anger of the Lord is past.
