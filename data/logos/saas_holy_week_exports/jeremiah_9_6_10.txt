9:6: Therefore thus says the Lord: “Behold, I will set them on fire, and test them; I shall do so because of the evil of the daughter of My people. 
9:7: Their tongue is a wounding arrow, and the words of their mouth are deceitful. One speaks peaceably to his neighbor, but has hatred in his heart. 
9:8: Shall I not visit them for these things?” says the Lord. “Shall not My soul take vengeance on such a people as this? 
9:9: Take up a wailing on the mountains, and a lamentation on the paths of the desert; for they cease from being a people. They do not hear the sound of existence from the birds of the sky, nor even from cattle. They were astonished; they were gone. 
9:10: I will put Jerusalem into captivity, and it will become a dwelling place of serpents. I will make the cities of Judah desolate, beyond habitation.”
