2:3: Therefore thus says the Lord: “Behold, I am devising evils against this tribe from which you shall not lift up your neck, nor will you suddenly walk upright, for this is a time of evil. 
2:4: In that day a parable shall be taken up against you, and a dirge will be wailed saying, ‘In misery we have suffered hardship. The portion of my people has been measured out with a measuring line, and there was no one who was able to stop him. Our fields have been divided among them. 
2:5: Therefore there will be no one to put out a measuring line for a lot for you in the assembly of the Lord.’ 
2:6: “Do not shed tears, nor even weep over these things; for He will not dismiss the reproaches, 
2:7: who says, “The house of Jacob has provoked the Spirit of the Lord; are these not His practices? Are not His words good with Him, and have they not proceeded as predicted? 
2:8: And previously, for enmity my people resisted against his peace. They flayed his skin to remove hope in the ravages of war. 
2:9: Therefore those leading my people will be cast out of their luxurious homes; they have been driven out because of their wicked practices. Draw near to the everlasting mountains.’ ” 
2:10: Arise and depart, for this is not a final resting place for you on account of uncleanness; you have been utterly destroyed by corruption. 
2:11: You were pursued by no one pursuing! You pursued a spirit built on a falsehood; it drips on you, into the wine and the intoxicating drink, and by reason of the drop of this people, it shall happen.
