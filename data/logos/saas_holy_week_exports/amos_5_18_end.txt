5:18: Woe to you who desire the day of the Lord! What is the day of the Lord to you? It will be darkness, and not light. 
5:19: It will be as though a man fled from a lion, and a bear met him! As though he rushed into the house, leaned his hand on the wall, and a serpent bit him! 
5:20: Is not the day of the Lord darkness and not light? Is it not a deep darkness, with no brightness in it? 
5:21: “I hate, I reject your feast days. I do not savor your sacred assemblies. 
5:22: Though you offer Me burnt offerings and meat offerings, I will not accept them. I will not regard your fattened peace offerings. 
5:23: Take away from Me the sound of your songs, for I will not hear your musical instruments. 
5:24: Instead, let justice run swiftly as does water from the mountain, and let righteousness be as a stream that cannot be crossed. 
5:25: “O house of Israel, did you offer Me sacrifices and offerings those forty years in the wilderness? 
5:26: You have carried the tabernacle of Moloch and the star of your god Raiphan, which you made for yourselves. 
5:27: Therefore I shall exile you beyond Damascus,” says the Lord—the Almighty God is His name.
