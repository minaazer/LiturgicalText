23:1: “Woe to the shepherds who scatter and destroy the sheep of My pasture!” 
23:2: Therefore thus says the Lord against those who tend My people: “You have scattered My sheep, and driven them out. You did not care for them. Behold, I shall punish you according to your evil practices. 
23:3: I will receive the remnant of My people from every land where I have driven them. I will establish them in their pasture, and they shall increase and be multiplied. 
23:4: I will set up shepherds over them who will feed them. They shall fear no more, nor be terrified,” says the Lord. 
23:5: “Behold, days are coming,” says the Lord, “when I will raise up for David the Righteous Orient, and a King shall reign. He will understand and bring about judgment and righteousness on the earth. 
23:6: In His days, Judah will be saved, and Israel shall dwell in confidence. This is His name by which the Lord will call him: ‘The Lord Our Righteousness.’ ”
