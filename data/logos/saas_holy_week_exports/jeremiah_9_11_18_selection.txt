9:11: Who is a wise man? Let him understand this. Let him declare to you by the word of the Lord to him, why the land was ravaged by fire, and became like a desert no one could pass through. 
9:12: The Lord answered me, “Because they forsook My law which I set before their face, and they did not hear My voice. 
9:13: Instead, they walked after what pleased their evil heart, and after the idols their fathers taught them.” 
9:14: Therefore, thus says the Lord God of Israel: “Behold, I will feed them as a matter of necessity, and give them the water of bitterness to drink. 
9:15: I will also scatter them among the Gentiles, amongst whom neither they nor their fathers are known. I shall send a sword after them, until I consume them with it.” 
9:16: Thus says the Lord: “Call for the mourning women, and let them come. Send for the wise women, and let them give utterance. 
9:17: Let them take up a lamentation over you. Let your eyes run with tears, and your eyelids flow with water. 
9:18: For a voice of wailing is heard from Zion, ‘How we have been afflicted and greatly ashamed, because we forsook the land and were cast out of our dwellings.’ ”
