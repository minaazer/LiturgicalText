24:1: Behold, the Lord is about to lay waste the inhabited earth, and He will make it desolate, lay bare its surface, and scatter those who dwell therein. 
24:2: And it shall be that the people shall be as the priest, the servant as the lord, the maiden as the mistress, the buyer as the seller, the lender as the borrower, and the debtor as the creditor. 
24:3: The earth shall be completely laid waste and utterly plundered, for the mouth of the Lord has spoken these things. 
24:4: The earth mourns and the inhabited earth is ruined; the lofty of the earth mourn. 
24:5: The earth acts lawlessly because of its inhabitants, for they transgressed the law and changed the ordinances, the everlasting covenant. 
24:6: Therefore a curse shall devour the earth, because those who dwell in it sinned. Therefore the inhabitants of the earth shall be poor, and few people will be left. 
24:7: The wine shall mourn, the vine shall mourn, and all the merryhearted will groan. 
24:8: The mirth of tambourines, the willfulness and wealth of the ungodly, and the sound of the harp have ceased. 
24:9: They were ashamed; they did not drink wine; strong drink is bitter to those who drink it. 
24:10: Every city was made desolate; every house is shut up, that none may go in. 
24:11: Wail for wine everywhere; all gladness of the earth is gone! 
24:12: Cities shall be left desolate, and abandoned houses will be destroyed. 
24:13: All these things shall be in the earth in the midst of the nations, in the manner someone would glean an olive tree. Thus they shall glean them when the harvest is done.
