32:7: But the Lord said to Moses, “Go quickly! Get down from here! For your people, whom you brought out of the land of Egypt, are transgressing the law. 
32:8: They turned aside quickly from the way I commanded them. They made themselves a calf, and are worshiping and sacrificing to it, and are saying, ‘These are your gods, O Israel, that brought you out of the land of Egypt.’ 
32:9: Now therefore, let Me be, that I may burn in wrath against them and consume them. 
32:10: Then I will make of you a great nation.” 
32:11: But Moses prayed before the Lord God, and said, “Why, O Lord, does Your wrath burn hot against Your people whom You brought out of the land of Egypt with great power and a mighty hand? 
32:12: Why should the Egyptians speak, and say, ‘He brought them out with evil intent, to kill them in the mountains, and to consume them from the face of the earth?’ Turn from Your fierce wrath, and be merciful to the wickedness of Your people. 
32:13: Remember Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob, Your servants, to whom You swore by Yourself, and said to them, ‘I will greatly multiply your seed as the stars of heaven for multitude; and all this land I spoke about, to give their seed, they shall inherit unto the ages.’ ” 
32:14: So the Lord granted mercy for the harm He said He would do to His people. 
32:15: Then Moses turned and went down from the mountain, with the two tablets of the testimony in his hands. They were stone tablets written on both sides, on one side and on the other.
