1:1: Love righteousness, you who judge on the earth. Think about the Lord in goodness, and seek Him with sincerity of heart; 
1:2: Because He is found by those who do not tempt Him, And He is manifest to those who do not disbelieve Him. 
1:3: For dishonest reasoning separates people from God, And when His power examines someone, it convicts the undiscerning; 
1:4: For wisdom will not enter the soul that plots evil, Nor will it dwell in a body involved in sin. 
1:5: For a holy spirit of discipline flees from deceit, And sends away undiscerning reasoning; It will put wrongdoing to shame when it comes near. 
1:6: For wisdom is a spirit that loves mankind, But she will punish a blasphemer because of his words; For God is the witness of his thoughts, The true examiner of his heart, and the hearer of his tongue; 
1:7: Because the Spirit of the Lord fills the world, And He who holds all things together knows what is said. 
1:8: Therefore no one who speaks unrighteous things Will escape notice, Nor will justice, when it cross-examines, pass him by. 
1:9: For there shall be a close examination Into the deliberations of an ungodly man, And a report of his words will come to the Lord As proof of his lawlessness;
