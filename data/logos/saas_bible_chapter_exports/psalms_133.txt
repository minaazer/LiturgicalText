133:1: An Ode of Ascents. Behold now, bless the Lord, All you servants of the Lord, who stand in the house of the Lord, In the courts of the house of our God. 
133:2: In the nights, lift up your hands to the holy of holies. And bless the Lord. 
133:3: May the Lord bless you from Zion, He who made heaven and earth.
