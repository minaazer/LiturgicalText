139:1: For the End; a Psalm by David. 
139:2: Deliver me, O Lord, from the evil man, Rescue me from the unjust man, 
139:3: Who devised wrongdoing in their heart; They arrayed themselves for war all day long; 
139:4: They sharpened their tongue like a serpent; The venom of asps was under their lips. (Pause) 
139:5: Keep me, O Lord, from the hand of the sinner; Deliver me from unjust men, Who plotted to trip up my steps; 
139:6: The arrogant hid a trap for me, And they stretched out ropes, as snares for my feet; They set a stumbling block for me near my path. (Pause) 
139:7: I said to the Lord, “You are my God; Give ear, O Lord, to the voice of my supplication. 
139:8: O Lord, O Lord, the power of my salvation, You overshadowed my head in the day of war. 
139:9: Because of my desire, O Lord, do not hand me over to the sinner; They plotted against me; do not forsake me, lest they be exalted. (Pause) 
139:10: As for the head of those who encircle me, The toil of their lips shall cover them. 
139:11: Coals shall fall upon them; You shall overthrow them with fire; Do not let them withstand hardships.” 
139:12: A talkative man shall not prosper on earth; Evils shall hunt the unjust man to destruction. 
139:13: I know the Lord will maintain the cause of the poor man And the justice of the needy. 
139:14: Surely the righteous shall give thanks to Your name, And the upright shall dwell in Your presence.
