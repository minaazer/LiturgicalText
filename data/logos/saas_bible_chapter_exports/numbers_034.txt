34:1: Now the Lord spoke to Moses, saying, 
34:2: “Command the children of Israel, and say to them, ‘You shall come into the land of Canaan, and it shall be your inheritance, the land of Canaan with its borders. 
34:3: ‘Your southern border shall be from the Desert of Sin to the border of Edom; so your southern border shall extend eastward to the end of the Salt Sea; 
34:4: and your borders shall go around from the southern side of the Ascent of Akrabbim, continue to Sin, and be on the south of Kadesh Barnea; then it shall go on to Hazar Addar, and continue to Azmon; 
34:5: and the border shall go around from Azmon to the Brook of Egypt, and its termination shall be the Sea. 
34:6: ‘As for the western borders, you shall have the Great Sea for a border; this shall be your western border. 
34:7: ‘Now this shall be your northern border: from the Great Sea you shall measure your border along the mountain range; 
34:8: and from the mountain range you shall measure your border to Hamath; and its termination shall be the borders of Zedad; 
34:9: and the border shall proceed to Ziphron, and its termination shall be Hazar Enan. This shall be your northern borders. 
34:10: ‘You shall measure your eastern borders from Hazar Enan to Shepham; 
34:11: and the borders shall go down from Shepham to Riblah on the east side of Ain; and the borders shall go down to Bela behind the Sea of Chinnereth from the east; 
34:12: and the borders shall go down along the Jordan, and its termination shall be the Salt Sea. This shall be your land with its surrounding borders.’ ” 
34:13: Then Moses commanded the children of Israel, saying, “This is the land you shall inherit by lot, in the manner the Lord ordered Moses to give it to the nine tribes and to the half-tribe of Manasseh. 
34:14: For the tribe of the sons of Reuben and the tribe of the sons of Gad according to the houses of their fathers and the half-tribe of Manasseh received their allotments. 
34:15: The two tribes and the half-tribe received their allotments beyond the Jordan, opposite Jericho from the south towards the east.” 
34:16: Again the Lord spoke to Moses, saying, 
34:17: “These are the names of the men who shall divide the land among you as an inheritance: Eleazar the priest and Joshua the son of Nun. 
34:18: You shall take rulers, one from every tribe to divide the land for the inheritance. 
34:19: Now these are the names of the men: from the tribe of Judah, Caleb the son of Jephunneh; 
34:20: from the tribe of Simeon, Shemuel the son of Ammihud; 
34:21: from the tribe of Benjamin, Elidad the son of Chislon; 
34:22: from the tribe of Dan a ruler, Bukki the son of Jogli; 
34:23: from the sons of Joseph’s tribe: a ruler from the tribe of the children of Manasseh, Hanniel the son of Ephod, 
34:24: a ruler from the tribe of the sons of Ephraim, Kemuel the son of Shiphtan; 
34:25: a ruler from the tribe of Zebulun, Elizaphan the son of Parnach; 
34:26: a ruler from the tribe of the sons of Issachar, Paltiel the son of Azzan; 
34:27: a ruler from the tribe of the sons of Asher, Ahihud the son of Shelomi; 
34:28: a ruler from the tribe of Naphtali, Pedahel the son of Ammihud.” 
34:29: These are the ones the Lord commanded to divide the inheritance among the children of Israel in the land of Canaan.
