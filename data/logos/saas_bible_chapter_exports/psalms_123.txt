123:1: An Ode of Ascents. “If the Lord had not been with us,” Let Israel now say, 
123:2: “If the Lord had not been with us, When men rose up against us, 
123:3: Then they would have swallowed us alive, When their anger raged against us, 
123:4: Then the water would have drowned us; Our soul would have passed through a torrent, 
123:5: Then our soul would have passed through A water that is overwhelming. 
123:6: Blessed is the Lord, who did not give us As prey to their teeth. 
123:7: Our soul was delivered like a sparrow From the snare of the hunters; The snare was broken, and we were delivered. 
123:8: Our help is in the name of the Lord, Who made heaven and earth.
