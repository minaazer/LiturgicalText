141:1: Understanding; by David, When He Was in the Cave; a Prayer. 
141:2: I cried to the Lord with my voice, With my voice I prayed to the Lord. 
141:3: I shall pour out my supplication before Him; I shall declare my affliction in His presence. 
141:4: When my spirit fainted within me, Then You knew my paths; For on the way I was going, they hid a trap for me. 
141:5: I looked on my right, and saw There was no one who knew me; Refuge failed me, And there was no one who cared for my soul. 
141:6: I cried to You, O Lord; I said, “You are my hope, My portion in the land of the living. 
141:7: Attend to my supplication, For I was humbled exceedingly; Deliver me from my persecutors, For they are stronger than I. 
141:8: Bring my soul out of prison, To give thanks to Your name, O Lord; The righteous shall wait for me, until You reward me.”
