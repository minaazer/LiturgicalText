15:1: He who fears the Lord will do this, And he who holds fast to the law will obtain wisdom. 
15:2: She will come to meet him like a mother, And accept him like the wife of one’s youth. 
15:3: She will feed him the bread of understanding, And give him the water of wisdom to drink. 
15:4: He will be supported by her and not fall; And he will hold on to her and not be put to shame. 
15:5: She will exalt him alongside his neighbors, And in the midst of the assembly she will open his mouth. 
15:6: He will find gladness and a crown of rejoicing, And will inherit an everlasting name. 
15:7: But foolish men will not find her, And sinful men will not see her. 
15:8: She is far from the arrogant, And lying men will not keep her in mind. 
15:9: Praise is not proper in the mouth of a sinner, Because it was not sent from the Lord. 
15:10: For praise should be spoken in wisdom, Then the Lord will prosper it. 
15:11: Do not say, “I fell away because of the Lord,” For He will not do what He hates. 
15:12: Do not say, “It was He who led me astray,” For He has no need of a sinful man. 
15:13: The Lord hates all abominations, And they are not loved by those who fear Him. 
15:14: He himself created man in the beginning, And left him in the counsel of his will. 
15:15: If you will, you will keep the commandments, And faithfully do His good pleasure. 
15:16: He has set before you fire and water; If you will, stretch forth your hand. 
15:17: Life and death are before mankind, And whichever he chooses, it will be given to him. 
15:18: For great is the wisdom of the Lord; He is mighty in power and sees all things. 
15:19: His eyes are upon those who fear Him, And He Himself knows every deed of man. 
15:20: He has commanded no one to be ungodly, And He has given no one license to sin.
