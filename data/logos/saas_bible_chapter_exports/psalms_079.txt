79:1: For the End, Concerning Things That Shall Be Changed; A Testimony for Asaph, a Psalm Concerning the Assyrian. 
79:2: Give heed, O You who shepherd Israel; Reveal Yourself, O You who lead Joseph like a flock, Who sit upon the cherubim. 
79:3: Raise up Your power Before Ephraim, Benjamin, and Manasseh, And come for our salvation. 
79:4: O God, convert us, And reveal Your face, and we shall be saved. 
79:5: O Lord God of hosts, How long will You be angry with the prayer of Your servant? 
79:6: Will You feed us the bread of tears, And will You give us as drink tears in measure? 
79:7: You made us an offence to our neighbors, And our enemies sneered at us. 
79:8: O Lord God, convert us, And reveal Your face, and we shall be saved. (Pause) 
79:9: You removed a vineyard from Egypt; You cast out the nations, and planted it; 
79:10: You prepared the way before it, And You planted its roots; and the earth was filled. 
79:11: Its shade covered the mountains, And its vines the cedars of God; 
79:12: Its branches reached to the sea, And its shoots as far as the river. 
79:13: Why did You pull down its hedge, And all who pass on the road gather its grapes? 
79:14: A wild swine from the forest laid it waste, A solitary wild beast devoured it. 
79:15: O God of hosts, convert us now; Look down from heaven and behold, And visit this vineyard 
79:16: Your right hand planted, and perfect it. And visit the son of man, whom You strengthened for Yourself. 
79:17: It was set on fire, and uprooted, But they shall perish at the rebuke of your face. 
79:18: Let Your hand be upon the man of Your right hand, And upon the son of man, whom You strengthened for Yourself; 
79:19: Then we will not turn away from You; You will give us life, and we will call upon Your name. 
79:20: O Lord God of hosts, convert us, and reveal Your face, and we shall be saved.
