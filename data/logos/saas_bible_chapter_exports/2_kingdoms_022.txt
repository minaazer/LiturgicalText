22:1: And David spoke to the Lord the words of this song on the day when the Lord delivered him from the hand of all his enemies, and from the hand of Saul. 
22:2: And he sang: “O Lord, my rock, my fortress and my deliverer; 
22:3: My God will be my guardian, in whom I will trust; My defender and the horn of my salvation, My protector and the refuge of my salvation; You will save me from unrighteousness. 
22:4: I will call upon the Lord, who is worthy to be praised; And I will be saved from my enemies. 
22:5: “When the troubles of death encompassed me, The floods of wickedness terrified me. 
22:6: The throes of death surrounded me; The harshness of death confronted me. 
22:7: In my distress I will call upon the Lord And cry out to my God; And He will hear my voice from His temple, And my cry will enter His ears. 
22:8: “Then the earth was shaken and trembled; And the foundations of heaven were troubled, Because the Lord was angry with them. 
22:9: From out of His wrath, a smoke went up, And from His mouth a devouring fire; Coals were kindled by it. 
22:10: He bowed the heavens and came down, And there was darkness under His feet. 
22:11: He rode upon the cherubim, and He did fly; He was seen upon the wings of the wind. 
22:12: He put darkness around Him as a hiding place. About Him, His tabernacle was the darkness of waters; And by the clouds of the air, He thickened it. 
22:13: From the brightness of His light Coals of fire were kindled. 
22:14: “The Lord thundered from heaven, And the Most High uttered His voice. 
22:15: He sent arrows and scattered them; He confounded them with lightning. 
22:16: The channels of the sea were seen, The foundations of the world were uncovered, With a blast of the wind, His wrath. 
22:17: “He sent from the height and took me, He drew me out of many waters. 
22:18: He delivered me from my strong enemies, From those who hated me; For they were stronger than I. 
22:19: They confronted me in the day of my tribulation, But the Lord was my support. 
22:20: He brought me to a broad place; He delivered me because He delighted in me. 
22:21: “The Lord repaid me according to my righteousness; According to the purity of my hands, He repaid me. 
22:22: For I have kept the ways of the Lord And have not wickedly departed from my God. 
22:23: For all His judgments were before me, And I did not depart from His statutes. 
22:24: I will be blameless before Him, And I will guard myself from my iniquity. 
22:25: And the Lord will recompense me according to my righteousness, According to the cleanness of my hands in His eyes. 
22:26: “With the holy You will be holy; With a perfect man You will be perfect; 
22:27: With the chosen You will be the choice one; And with the devious You will be shrewd. 
22:28: You will save the needy people; But You will humble the eyes of the haughty. 
22:29: “For You, O Lord, are my lamp; The Lord shall enlighten my darkness. 
22:30: For by You I can run against a band of troops; By my God I can leap over a wall. 
22:31: As for the Mighty One, His way is blameless; The word of the Lord is strong, tried by fire; He is a defender to all who trust in Him. 
22:32: “Who is mighty except the Lord? And who will be the Creator, except our God? 
22:33: The Mighty One who strengthens me with power, Who has prepared my way blameless. 
22:34: He makes my feet like the feet of deer And sets me on my high places. 
22:35: He instructs my hands for war, So that my arms can bend a bow of bronze. 
22:36: “You have given me the protection of my salvation; And Your gentleness fulfills me. 
22:37: My pathway under me is widened, And my legs are not shaken. 
22:38: I shall pursue my enemies and destroy them, And I will not turn back until I destroy them. 
22:39: I will break them and they will not rise, For they will fall under my feet. 
22:40: For You will strengthen me with power for the battle; You will subdue under me those who rose against me. 
22:41: You have given me the back of my enemy; So I killed those who hated me. 
22:42: They will cry out, but no one will help; Even to the Lord, but He did not listen to them. 
22:43: Then I beat them as fine as the dust of the earth; I crushed them like dirt in the streets. 
22:44: “You will deliver me from the quarrels of the people; You have kept me at the head of the nations. A people I have not known served me. 
22:45: Philistines deceived me; As soon as they heard, they obeyed me. 
22:46: Philistines shall be cast out, And they will stumble out from their hideouts. 
22:47: “The Lord lives! Blessed be my Guardian! Let my God be exalted, The Guardian of my salvation! 
22:48: It is the Mighty Lord who avenges me And chastises the peoples under me; 
22:49: He delivers me from my enemies. You will lift me above those who rise against me; You will deliver me from the man of injustice. 
22:50: Therefore, O Lord, I will confess You to the Gentiles And sing praises to Your name. 
22:51: “He is the one who extols the salvation of His king And shows mercy to His anointed, To David and his seed forever.”
