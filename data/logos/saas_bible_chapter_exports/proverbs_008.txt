8:1: You shall proclaim wisdom, That discernment may obey you; 
8:2: For she is on the lofty summit, And stands in the midst of the paths. 
8:3: For she sits beside the gates of princes, And sings at the entrances: 
8:4: “You, O men, I exhort, And I utter my voice to the sons of men; 
8:5: Understand astuteness, O simple ones; And put it in your heart, O uninstructed ones. 
8:6: Obey me, for I speak sacred things, And from my lips I will bring forth things that are true. 
8:7: For my mouth shall speak the truth, But false lips are an abomination before me. 
8:8: All the words of my mouth are with righteousness; There is nothing in them crooked or twisted. 
8:9: All things are evident to those who understand, And true to those who find knowledge. 
8:10: Lay hold of instruction and not silver, And knowledge rather than tested gold, And choose perception rather than pure gold. 
8:11: For wisdom is better than precious stones, And every costly thing is not worthy of her. 
8:12: “I, wisdom, dwell with counsel, And I call upon knowledge and understanding. 
8:13: The fear of the Lord hates unrighteousness, And both rudeness and arrogance, and the ways of wicked men; And I hate the perverted ways of evil men. 
8:14: Counsel and safety are mine; Discernment is mine, and strength is mine; 
8:15: By me, kings reign, And princes write of righteousness; 
8:16: By me, noblemen become great, And by me, monarchs have power in the land. 
8:17: I love those who love me, And those who seek me shall find me. 
8:18: Riches and glory belong to me, And abundant possessions, and righteousness; 
8:19: It is better to enjoy my fruits than gold and precious stones, And my fruits are better than choice silver. 
8:20: I walk in the ways of righteousness, And I am conversant with the paths of judgment; 
8:21: That I may distribute substance to those who love me, And may fill their treasuries with good things. 
8:22: “If I declare to you the things that happen daily, I will also remember to recount the things of old. 
8:23: The Lord created me the beginning of His ways for His works; He established me in the beginning before time, 
8:24: Before He made the earth, and before He made the abysses, Before the going forth of the fountains of waters, 
8:25: Before the mountains were created; And He beget me before all hills. 
8:26: The Lord made the fields and the uninhabited places, And the inhabited heights under heaven. 
8:27: When He prepared heaven, I was present with Him, And when He set apart His throne upon the winds. 
8:28: When He made strong the things above the clouds, And made sure the fountains under heaven, 
8:29: And made strong the foundations of the earth, I was working beside Him; 
8:30: I was He in whom He rejoiced; Daily and continually I was gladdened by His face. 
8:31: When He completed the world, He rejoiced, And He rejoiced in the sons of men. 
8:32: “Now, therefore, my son, hear me: Blessed is the man who shall hearken to me, And the man who shall guard my ways; 
8:33: Who watches daily at my doors, Who keeps watch at the doors of my entryways; 
8:34: For my issues are the issues of life, And grace is prepared from the Lord. 
8:35: But those who sin against me act ungodly toward their own souls, And those who hate me love death.”
