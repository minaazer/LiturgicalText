38:1: After Elihu had ceased from speaking, then the Lord spoke to Job out of the whirlwind and clouds, saying: 
38:2: “Who is this who hides counsel from Me, And holds words in his heart, And thinks to conceal them from Me? 
38:3: Gird your waist like a man; I will question you, and you shall answer Me. 
38:4: “Where were you when I laid the foundations of the earth? Tell Me, if you have understanding. 
38:5: Who determined its measurements? Surely you know. Or who stretched the line upon it? 
38:6: To what were its foundations fastened? Or who laid its cornerstone, 
38:7: When the stars were made, And all my angels praised me in a loud voice? 
38:8: “I shut up the sea with doors, When it burst forth and issued from the womb. 
38:9: I made the clouds its garment, and wrapped it in mist. 
38:10: I fixed My limit for it, and set bars and doors. 
38:11: I said, ‘This far you may come, but no further, And here your waves must stop.’ 
38:12: “Did I order the morning light in your time, And did the morning star see its appointed place, 
38:13: To lay hold of the ends of the earth, and to shake the ungodly out of it? 
38:14: Or did you take clay of the ground, and form a living creature, And set it with the power of speech upon the earth? 
38:15: Have you removed light from the ungodly, And crushed the arm of the arrogant? 
38:16: “Have you come upon the fountain of the sea, And walked in the tracks of the deep? 
38:17: Do the gates of death open to you for fear, And did the doorkeepers of Hades quake when they saw you? 
38:18: Have you been instructed in the breadth of the earth under heaven? Tell Me now, what is the extent of it? 
38:19: “In what kind of land does the light dwell, And what kind is the place of darkness? 
38:20: If you would bring me to their boundaries, And also if you understand their paths, 
38:21: Then I know you were born at that time, And the number of your years is great. 
38:22: “Have you come upon the storehouses of snow? Have you seen the storehouses of hail? 
38:23: Are they stored for you against the time of your enemies, For the day of wars and battles? 
38:24: From where does the frost proceed? From where does the south wind scatter over the earth? 
38:25: “Who prepared a course for the violent rain? Or a path for the thunders? 
38:26: Who causes it to rain on a land where there is no man, Or an uninhabited wilderness, where there is not a man in it, 
38:27: To satisfy the untrodden and uninhabited land, And cause it to spring forth a crop of tender grass? 
38:28: Who is the rain’s father, And who has begotten the drops of dew? 
38:29: From whose womb comes the ice, And who has given birth to the frost in heaven, 
38:30: Which descends like flowing water? Who made firm the face of the abyss? 
38:31: “Do you understand the band of the Pleiades, And have you opened the fence of Orion? 
38:32: Will you open up Mazzaroth in its season, And the Evening Star with his rays? Will you guide them? 
38:33: Do you know the movements of heaven, Or the events which take place together under heaven? 
38:34: “Will you call a cloud with your voice, And will it obey you with a violent shower of much rain? 
38:35: Can you send out lightnings, and they shall go? They shall say to you, ‘What do you want?’ 
38:36: Who has given to women wisdom in weaving, Or knowledge of embroidery? 
38:37: Who can number the clouds by wisdom, And who bowed heaven down to earth? 
38:38: For it is spread like a cloud of dust, And I have joined it together as one hewn stone to another. 
38:39: “Will you hunt prey for the lions, Or satisfy the lives of the serpents? 
38:40: They fear in their dens, And live in the forests, and lie in wait. 
38:41: Who provides food for the raven? For its young ones wander, And cry to the Lord in search of food.
