5:1: “Hear these things, O priests, And attend, O house of Israel! Listen, O house of the king! For judgment is with you Because you have been a snare in Mizpah And a net spread on Tabor, 
5:2: Which they that hunt the prey have fixed; But I will be your instructor. 
5:3: I know Ephraim, and Israel is not far from Me; For now Ephraim has gone grievously a-whoring; Israel is defiled. 
5:4: “Their deliberations did not allow them To return to their God, For a spirit of fornication is in them, And they have not gotten to know the Lord. 
5:5: The arrogance of Israel Shall be brought low before His face, And Israel and Ephraim shall become weak Due to their wrongdoings; And Judah also shall become weak with them. 
5:6: “They shall go with sheep and calves Diligently to seek the Lord; But they shall not find Him, For He has withdrawn Himself from them. 
5:7: For they have forsaken the Lord Because strange children have been born to them. Now the rust shall devour them along with their heritages. 
5:8: “Blow the trumpet on the hills, Sound aloud on the heights, Proclaim in Beth Aven: ‘Benjam in is confused.’ 
5:9: Ephraim has vanished in the days of reproof; Among the tribes of Israel I have shown faithful things. 
5:10: “The princes of Judah became as those Who remove the boundaries; I will pour out my fury upon them as water. 
5:11: Ephraim oppressed his adversary; He trod over judgment, For he began to go after vanities. 
5:12: Therefore I will be as a confusion to Ephraim And as a sting to the house of Judah. 
5:13: “Ephraim saw his disease And Judah his pain; Then Ephraim went to the Assyrians And sent ambassadors to King Jareb. But he could not heal you, And your pain shall in no way cease from you. 
5:14: Wherefore I am as a panther to Ephraim And as a lion to the house of Judah. So I will grasp and go away; And I will take, and there shall be none to rescue. 
5:15: I will go and return to My place Until they are destroyed, And then they shall seek My face.
