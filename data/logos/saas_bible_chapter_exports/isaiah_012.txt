12:1: And in that day, you will say, “I will bless You, O Lord. Although You were angry with me, You turned away Your anger and had mercy on me. 
12:2: Behold, God is my Savior and Lord. I will trust in Him and be saved by Him. I will not be afraid, for the Lord is my glory and my praise. He has become my salvation.” 
12:3: You will draw water with gladness from the wells of salvation. 
12:4: In that day, you will say, “Praise the Lord; call upon His name. Declare His glorious things among the Gentiles and make mention that His name is exalted. 
12:5: Sing to the name of the Lord, for He has done great things. Declare this in all the earth. 
12:6: Exult and be glad, O inhabitants of Zion, for the Holy One of Israel is exalted in her midst.”
