41:1: “Have you not seen him? Have you not wondered at what has been said of him? 
41:2: Do you not fear because I am prepared? Who then is able to stand against Me? 
41:3: Who will stand against Me and endure, Since everything under heaven is Mine? 
41:4: “I will not be silent because of him, Though because of his power, one shall pity his antagonist. 
41:5: Who will remove his outer coat? Who may enter within the fold of his breast plate? 
41:6: Who will open the doors of his face, With his terrible teeth all around? 
41:7: His entrails are as brazen shields, And the texture of his skin as a symrite stone. 
41:8: One part clings to another, So no air comes between them. 
41:9: A man will be joined to his brother. They stick together, and cannot be parted. 
41:10: His sneezings flash forth light, And his eyes are like the appearance of the morning star. 
41:11: Out of his mouth go burning lights; Sparks of fire shoot out. 
41:12: Smoke goes out of his nostrils, As from a boiling pot with fire of coals. 
41:13: His breath kindles coals, And a flame goes out of his mouth. 
41:14: Strength dwells in his neck, And destruction runs before him. 
41:15: The muscles of his body are joined together. They are firm on him and cannot be moved. 
41:16: His heart is as hard as stone, And it stands like an unyielding anvil. 
41:17: When he turns, he is a terror To the four-footed wild animals which leap upon the earth. 
41:18: Though the sword reaches him, it cannot avail; Nor does spear or javelin. 
41:19: He regards iron as straw, And bronze as rotten wood. 
41:20: The arrow cannot wound him; The sling-stone becomes like grass to him. 
41:21: Hammers are regarded as straw, And he laughs at the threat of javelins. 
41:22: His bed is like sharp skewers, and all the gold of the sea under him Is like a great amount of clay. 
41:23: He makes the deep boil like a brazen pot, And he regards the sea like a pot of ointment. 
41:24: The lowest part of the deep is as a captive. He reckons the deep as his walking range. 
41:25: On earth there is nothing like him, Who was made to be mocked at by My angels. 
41:26: He beholds every high thing; He is king over all that are in the waters.”
