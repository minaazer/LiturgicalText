37:1: “At this also my heart was troubled, and flows from its place. 
37:2: Listen attentively to the thunder of the Lord’s wrath, And the discourse coming from His mouth. 
37:3: His dominion is under the whole heaven, And His light to the ends of the earth. 
37:4: Behind Him a voice will cry aloud. He will thunder with His voice of outrage, Yet He will not change them when His voice is heard. 
37:5: The Mighty One will thunder marvelously with His voice, For He does great things we cannot comprehend. 
37:6: For He commands the snow, ‘Fall upon the earth,’ And likewise, there is the winter, The rain and the winter-storms of His might. 
37:7: He seals up the hand of every man, That everyone may know his weakness. 
37:8: The wild animals go into their shelters, And are at rest in their lairs. 
37:9: From the secret chambers come pains, And cold from the mountaintop. 
37:10: By the breath of the mighty wind, He will send frost, And He freezes the waters as He wishes. 
37:11: And when the cloud obscures what He chooses, His light will disperse the cloud. 
37:12: He will swirl the encircling clouds by His governance, To perform all their works, moving them to do whatever He commands. This has been appointed by Him on the earth, 
37:13: Whether for correction, or for His earth, Or if He shall find him an object for mercy. 
37:14: “Pay close attention to this, O Job. Stand still and consider the power of the Lord. 
37:15: We know that God has appointed His works, Having made light out of darkness. 
37:16: He knows the division of the clouds, And the violent falls of evil men. 
37:17: But your robe is warm, And there is quiet upon the land. 
37:18: Will you establish with Him the foundations For the ancient heavens? They are strong like a molten mirror. 
37:19: “What is this to you? Teach me what we will say to Him, And let us cease speaking many words. 
37:20: Have I a book or scribe by me, That I may stand and put man to silence? 
37:21: But the light is not visible to all. It shines far off in the ancient heavens, As that which is from Him in the clouds. 
37:22: From the north come the clouds, shining like gold; In these great things are the glory and honor of the Almighty. 
37:23: We do not find another like Him in His strength. Do you not think He who judges righteously listens? 
37:24: Therefore men shall fear Him, And the wise in heart shall fear Him.”
