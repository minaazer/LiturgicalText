46:1: For the End; a Psalm for the Sons of Korah. 
46:2: Oh, clap your hands, all you nations; Shout to God with the voice of rejoicing; 
46:3: For the Lord Most High is fearsome, A great King over all the earth. 
46:4: He subdued the peoples under us, And the nations under our feet. 
46:5: He chose us for His inheritance, Jacob’s beauty, which He loved. (Pause) 
46:6: God ascended with a shout, The Lord with the sound of the trumpet. 
46:7: Sing praises to God; sing praises; Sing praises to our King; sing praises; 
46:8: For God is King of all the earth; Sing praises with understanding. 
46:9: God reigns over the nations; God sits on His holy throne. 
46:10: The rulers of the people gather together With the God of Abraham, For God’s mighty ones of the earth are greatly exalted.
