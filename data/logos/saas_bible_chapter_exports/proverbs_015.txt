15:1: Wrath destroys even men of discernment, But a humble answer turns away anger; And a grievous word stirs up wrath. 
15:2: The tongue of the wise understands good things, But the mouth of those without discernment proclaims evil things. 
15:3: The eyes of the Lord are in every place; They keep watch over both the evil and the good. 
15:4: The healing of the tongue is a tree of life, And the one who guards it will be filled with the Spirit. 
15:5: A son without discernment treats the instruction of his father with contempt, But the one who guards his commandments is more astute. 
15:6: In abundant righteousness there is much strength, But the ungodly shall utterly perish from the earth. 
15:7: In the houses of the righteous there is much strength, But the fruits of the ungodly shall perish. 
15:8: The lips of the wise are bound by perception, But the hearts of those without discernment are not safe. 
15:9: The sacrifices of the ungodly are an abomination to the Lord, But the prayers of the upright are acceptable with Him. 
15:10: The ways of the ungodly are an abomination to the Lord, But He loves those who pursue righteousness. 
15:11: The instruction of the simple is known by those who pass by, But those who hate reproofs will die shamefully. 
15:12: Hades and destruction are manifest before the Lord; How can the hearts of men not be also? 
15:13: An uninstructed man will not love those who reprove him, And he will not keep company with the wise. 
15:14: When one’s heart is glad, his face is cheerful, But when it is in grief, his face is sad. 
15:15: The upright heart seeks perception, But the mouth of the uninstructed will know evil things. 
15:16: The eyes of evil men are waiting for evil things all the time, But good men are at rest continually. 
15:17: Better is a small portion with the fear of the Lord, Than great treasures with lack of fear. 
15:18: Better is a dinner of herbs with friendship and grace, Than a fatted calf with hostility. 
15:19: An angry man stirs up contention, But a longsuffering man even calms the rising quarrel. 
15:20: A longsuffering man will extinguish condemnations, But the ungodly man stirs up more. 
15:21: The ways of idle men are strewn with thorny plants, But the thorny plants of the courageous are worn smooth. 
15:22: A wise son gladdens a father, But a son without discernment treats his mother with contempt. 
15:23: The paths of one without understanding are in need of discernment, But a man with discernment walks uprightly. 
15:24: Those not honoring councils defer their deliberations, But counsel remains in the hearts of those who resolve to do it. 
15:25: The evil man will not obey counsel, Nor will he speak anything appropriate, even something for the common good. 
15:26: The thoughts of a wise man are his ways of life, That he might turn aside, and be saved from Hades. 
15:27: The Lord will destroy the houses of the arrogant, But He will strengthen the boundary of a widow. 
15:28: The thinking of the unrighteous man is an abomination to the Lord, But the words of the holy are sacred. 
15:29: He who receives bribes utterly destroys himself, But he who hates the receiving of bribes saves himself. 
15:30: Sins are cleansed by almsgiving and faithfulness, And by the fear of the Lord everyone turns aside from evil. 
15:31: The hearts of the righteous care about faithfulness, But the mouth of the ungodly answers evil things. 
15:32: The ways of righteous men are acceptable before the Lord, And through them even enemies become friends. 
15:33: God is far from the ungodly, But He hears the prayers of the righteous. 
15:34: Better is a small receipt with the righteous, Than abundant fruits with the unrighteous. 
15:35: Let the heart of a man think righteous things, That he may make his steps straight under God. 
15:36: The eye that beholds good things gladdens the heart, But a good report enriches the bones. 
15:37: He who rejects instruction hates himself, But he who gives heed to reproofs loves his soul. 
15:38: The fear of God is instruction and wisdom, And the beginning of glory will respond to it.
