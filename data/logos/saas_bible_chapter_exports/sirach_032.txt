32:1: Did they appoint you as leader of the banquet? Do not exalt yourself. Among them be as one of them. Take care of them and only then be seated. 
32:2: Then having fulfilled all your duties, take your place, That you may make merry for their sake, And receive a crown because of your orderly behavior. 
32:3: Speak, O elder, for this is fitting for you to do so with accurate knowledge; But do not interrupt the music. 
32:4: Where there is entertainment, do not pour out talk, And do not display your cleverness at the wrong time. 
32:5: A concert of music at a banquet of wine Is like a seal of ruby in a gold ornament. 
32:6: A seal of emerald in a gold setting Is the melody of music with delicious wine. 
32:7: Speak, young man, if there is need of you, But no more than twice, and only if you are asked repeatedly. 
32:8: Be brief; say much in a few words. Act like a man who knows yet holds his tongue. 
32:9: Among the great do not act as their equal, And when another is speaking, do little talking. 
32:10: Lightning flashes before thunder, And favor goes before a modest man. 
32:11: Rise to leave in good time, and do not lag behind. Run home and do not be remiss. 
32:12: Amuse yourself there, and do what you intend, But do not sin by speaking arrogantly; 
32:13: And for these things, bless Him who created you, And satisfies you with His good things. 
32:14: The man who fears the Lord will receive instruction, And those who rise early will find His approval. 
32:15: He who seeks the Law will be filled with it, But the hypocrite will be stumbled by it. 
32:16: Those who fear the Lord will discover judgment, And like the light they will kindle righteous deeds. 
32:17: A sinful man will turn aside correction, And find a rationalization according to his will. 
32:18: A man of counsel will not neglect thought, But a strange and arrogant man does not tremble in fear. 
32:19: Do nothing without counsel, And when you have acted, do not regret it. 
32:20: Do not walk a path filled with obstacles, And do not trip over stony ground. 
32:21: Do not put your trust in an unexplored way, 
32:22: And keep watch because of your children. 
32:23: In every work entrust your soul to the Lord, For this is the keeping of the commandments. 
32:24: He who believes in the Law heeds the commandments, And he who trusts in the Lord will not suffer loss.
