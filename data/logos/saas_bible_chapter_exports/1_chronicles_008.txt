8:1: Now Benjamin begot Bela his firstborn, Ashbel the second, Aharah the third, 
8:2: Nohah the fourth, and Rapha the fifth. 
8:3: The sons of Bela were Addar, Gera, Abihud, 
8:4: Abishua, Naaman, Ahoah, 
8:5: Gera, Shephuphan, and Huram. 
8:6: These are the sons of Ehud, who were the heads of the houses of their fathers, of the inhabitants of Geba—they who forced them to move to Manahath: 
8:7: Naaman, Ahijah, and Gera, who forced them to move. He begot Uzza and Ahihud. 
8:8: Also Shaharaim had children in the country of Moab, after he had sent away Hushim and Baara his wives. 
8:9: By Hodesh his wife he begot Jobab, Zibia, Mesha, Malcam, 
8:10: Jeuz, Sachiah, and Mirmah. These were his sons, heads of their fathers’ houses. 
8:11: And by Hushim he begot Abitub and Elpaal. 
8:12: The sons of Elpaal were Eber, Misham, and Shemed, who built Ono and Lod with its towns; 
8:13: and Beriah and Shema, who were heads of their fathers’ houses of the inhabitants of Aijalon, who drove out the inhabitants of Gath. 
8:14: Ahio, Shashak, Jeremoth, 
8:15: Zebadiah, Arad, Eder, 
8:16: Michael, Ispah, and Joha were the sons of Beriah. 
8:17: Zebadiah, Meshullam, Hizki, Heber, 
8:18: Ishmerai, Jizliah, and Jobab were the sons of Elpaal. 
8:19: Jakim, Zichri, Zabdi, 
8:20: Elienai, Zillethai, Eliel, 
8:21: Adaiah, Beraiah, and Shimrath were the sons of Shimei. 
8:22: Ishpan, Eber, Eliel, 
8:23: Abdon, Zichri, Hanan, 
8:24: Hananiah, Elam, Antothijah, 
8:25: Iphdeiah, and Penuel were the sons of Shashak. 
8:26: Shamsherai, Shehariah, Athaliah, 
8:27: Jaareshiah, Elijah, and Zichri were the sons of Jeroham. 
8:28: These were heads of the houses of their fathers by their generations, chief men. These dwelt in Jerusalem. 
8:29: Now the father of Gibeon, whose wife’s name was Maacah, dwelt at Gibeon. 
8:30: And his firstborn son was Abdon, then Zur, Kish, Baal, Nadab, 
8:31: Gedor, Ahio, Zecher, 
8:32: and Mikloth, who begot Shimeah. They also dwelt alongside their relatives in Jerusalem, with their brethren. 
8:33: Ner begot Kish, Kish begot Saul, and Saul begot Jonathan, Malchishua, Abinadab, and Esh-Baal. 
8:34: The son of Jonathan was Merib-Baal, and Merib-Baal begot Micah. 
8:35: The sons of Micah were Pithon, Melech, Tarea, and Ahaz. 
8:36: And Ahaz begot Jehoaddah; Jehoaddah begot Alemeth, Azmaveth, and Zimri; and Zimri begot Moza. 
8:37: Moza begot Binea, Raphah his son, Eleasah his son, and Azel his son. 
8:38: Azel had six sons whose names were these: Azrikam, Bocheru, Ishmael, Sheariah, Obadiah, and Hanan. All these were the sons of Azel. 
8:39: And the sons of Eshek his brother were Ulam his firstborn, Jeush the second, and Eliphelet the third. 
8:40: The sons of Ulam were mighty men of valor, archers. They had many sons and grandsons, one hundred fifty in all. These were all sons of Benjamin.
