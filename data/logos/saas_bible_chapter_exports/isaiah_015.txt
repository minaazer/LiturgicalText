15:1: The word against the land of Moab: In the night the land of Moab shall be destroyed, for the wall of the land of Moab shall be destroyed in the night. 
15:2: Lament for yourselves, for even Dibon, where your altar is, shall be destroyed. Go there to weep over Nebo in the land of Moab. Wail, for baldness shall be on every head, and all arms shall be mutilated. 
15:3: In her streets, dress yourselves with sackcloth and strike yourselves on her housetops. All of you cry out with bitter wailing in her streets. 
15:4: Heshbon and Elealeh cry out until their voice is heard as far as Jahaz; therefore, the loins of the land of Moab cry aloud, for its soul shall know this. 
15:5: The heart of the land of Moab cries aloud in itself as far as Zoar, like a three-year-old heifer. On the ascent of Luhith, they shall go up to you with weeping by way of Horonaim. She cries, “Destruction and earthquake!” 
15:6: The water of Nemerim shall be a desert, and its grass shall fail, for there will be no green grass. 
15:7: “Even so, will Moab be saved? For I will bring the Arabs upon the valley, and they shall take it. 
15:8: For the cry reaches the border of the land of Moab, even of Eglaim, and its wailing as far as the well of Elim. 
15:9: For the water of Dimon shall be full of blood, for I will bring Arabs upon Dimon. I shall take away the seed of Moab, Ariel and the remnant of Adama.
