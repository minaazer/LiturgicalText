21:1: Then Job answered and said: 
21:2: “Listen carefully to my words, That I may not have this consolation from you. 
21:3: Raise me and I will speak, Then you will stop laughing at me. 
21:4: “What? Is there any one who does not blame me? Or why should I not be furious? 
21:5: Look at me, and be astonished; And put your hand over your mouth. 
21:6: For even when I remember, I am alarmed, And pains take hold of my flesh. 
21:7: Why do the ungodly live and become old, even in wealth? 
21:8: Their offspring is according to their desire, And their children are before their eyes. 
21:9: Their houses flourish, and they have no cause for fear; Neither is there any affliction from the Lord upon them. 
21:10: Their cow does not miscarry; For it is preserved safe during pregnancy, And does not suffer a miscarriage. 
21:11: They remain as an unfailing herd, And their children dance, 
21:12: Taking up the psaltery and harp; And they celebrate at the sound of song. 
21:13: They spend their life in their good things, And fall asleep in the rest of the grave. 
21:14: Yet such a man says to the Lord, ‘Depart from me. I do not wish to know Your ways. 
21:15: What is a sufficient reason for us to serve Him? What benefit is it for us to meet Him? 
21:16: For their good things are in their power, For He does not take notice of the works of the ungodly.’ 
21:17: “Nevertheless, the lamp of the ungodly will be put out. Destruction shall come upon them, And the sorrows of wrath will seize them. 
21:18: They are like chaff before the wind, And they are like dust a storm carries away. 
21:19: May his possessions fail to supply his children. God will recompense him, and he will not know it. 
21:20: May his eyes see his own slaughter, And may he not be saved by the Lord, 
21:21: For Is his will is not in his house with himself, But the number of his months has been apportioned. 
21:22: “Is it not the Lord who teaches understanding and knowledge? Does not He judge murders? 
21:23: One shall die in the strength of his simplicity, And wholly at ease and prosperous. 
21:24: His inwards are full of stiff fat, And the marrow of his bones is dissipated. 
21:25: Another dies under bitterness of soul, Not eating any good thing. 
21:26: They lie down together in the earth, And decay covers them. 
21:27: Thus I know you press me recklessly. 
21:28: You will say, ‘Where is the house of the ruler? Where is the shelter of the dwelling place of the ungodly?’ 
21:29: Ask those who travel the road, And do not overlook their signs. 
21:30: For the evil man makes light of the day of destruction, But they shall be led away in the day of His wrath. 
21:31: Who will tell him his way to his face? Who will recompense him for what he has done? 
21:32: Yet he is brought to the graves, And a vigil is kept over the coffin. 
21:33: The pebbles of the valley have been sweet to him. Every man shall depart after him, as countless have departed before him. 
21:34: How then can you comfort me with empty words, Since I have no rest from you?”
