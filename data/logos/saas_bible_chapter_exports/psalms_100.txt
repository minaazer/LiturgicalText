100:1: A Psalm by David. O Lord, I will sing to You of mercy and judgment; 
100:2: I will sing and understand in a blameless way; when will You come to me? I walked in the innocence of my heart in the midst of my house. 
100:3: I have not set any lawless thing before my eyes; I hated those who commit transgressions; 
100:4: A crooked heart shall not cleave to me; As for the evil man who turned away from me, I did not know him. 
100:5: He who secretly slanders his neighbor, this one I chased away; The one with an arrogant eye and insatiable heart, with this one I did not eat. 
100:6: My eyes were on the faithful of the earth, so they might dwell with me; He who walks in a blameless way, this one ministered to me. 
100:7: He who acts arrogantly did not dwell in the midst of my house; He who speaks unjustly did not prosper before my eyes. 
100:8: In the morning I slew all the sinners of the earth, So as to destroy from the city of the Lord all the workers of lawlessness.
