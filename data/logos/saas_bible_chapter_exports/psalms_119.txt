119:1: An Ode of Ascents. To the Lord in my affliction, I cried out, and He heard me. 
119:2: O Lord, deliver my soul from unjust lips And from a deceitful tongue. 
119:3: What should be given You, and what added to You, Against a deceitful tongue? 
119:4: The arrows of the Mighty One are sharpened With hot coals from the desert. 
119:5: Woe is me! My sojourning was prolonged; I dwelt with the tents of Kedar. 
119:6: My soul sojourned a long time as a resident alien. 
119:7: With those who hate peace, I was peaceful; When I spoke to them, they made war against me without a cause.
