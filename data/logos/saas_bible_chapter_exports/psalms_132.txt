132:1: An Ode of Ascents; by David. Behold now, what is so good or so pleasant As for brothers to dwell together in unity? 
132:2: It is like fragrant oil running down upon the beard, The beard of Aaron, Running down upon the border of his garment; 
132:3: It is like the dew of Hermon, running down upon the mountains of Zion; For there the Lord commanded the blessing And life forever.
