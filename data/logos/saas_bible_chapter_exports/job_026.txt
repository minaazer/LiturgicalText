26:1: But Job answered and said: 
26:2: “To whom are you attached? Or whom are you going to help? Is it not He who has great strength, And whose arm is powerful? 
26:3: To whom have you given counsel? Is it not to Him who has all wisdom? Or whom will you follow? Is it not One who has the greatest power? 
26:4: To whom have you spoken words? Whose breath came forth from you? 
26:5: “Will giants be born under the water And those inhabiting them? 
26:6: Hades is naked before Him, And destruction has no covering. 
26:7: He stretches out the north wind upon nothing; He hangs the earth on nothing. 
26:8: He binds up the water in His clouds, Yet the cloud is not broken under it. 
26:9: He grasps the face of His throne, And spreads His cloud over it. 
26:10: He encompassed the face of the water by an ordinance, Until the end of light and darkness. 
26:11: The pillars of heaven were spread all over, And stood amazed at His rebuke. 
26:12: He checked the sea with His power, And by His understanding He wounded the sea-monster. 
26:13: For the bars of heaven fear Him, Because He put to death the rebellious dragon by a command. 
26:14: Behold, these are parts of His way, And we will hearken to the least of His words. But who knows when He will bring about the strength of His thunder?”
