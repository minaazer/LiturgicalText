99:1: A Psalm of Thanksgiving. Shout aloud to the Lord, all the earth; 
99:2: Serve the Lord with gladness; Come before Him with great joy. 
99:3: Know this: the Lord, He is God; He made us, and not we ourselves; We are His people and the sheep of His pasture. 
99:4: Enter into His gates with thanksgiving, And into His courts with hymns; Give thanks to Him; praise His name; 
99:5: For the Lord is good; His mercy is everlasting, And His truth is from generation to generation.
