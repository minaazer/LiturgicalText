10:1: A wise son makes his father glad, But a son without discernment is a grief to his mother. 
10:2: Treasures do not benefit lawless men, But righteousness delivers from death. 
10:3: The Lord will not let the soul of the righteous man starve, But He overthrows the life of the ungodly. 
10:4: Poverty humbles a man, But the hands of courageous men enrich others. 
10:5: A son who is instructed will be wise, And he will use a man without discernment as a servant. 
10:6: A son with understanding is kept safe from the heat, But a lawless son is blasted by the wind at harvest time. 
10:7: The blessing of the Lord is upon the head of a righteous man, But untimely grief will cover the mouth of the ungodly. 
10:8: The remembrance of the righteous is with eulogies, But the name of an ungodly man is extinguished. 
10:9: The wise man in heart will receive the commandments, But the man unguarded in speech will be overthrown in his perverseness. 
10:10: He who walks in sincerity walks confidently, But he who perverts his ways will be known. 
10:11: He who winks his eyes deceitfully gathers grief for men, But he who reproves boldly is a peacemaker. 
10:12: A fountain of life is in the hand of a righteous man, But the mouth of an ungodly man will hide destruction. 
10:13: Hatred stirs up strife, But love covers all who are not lovers of strife. 
10:14: He who brings forth wisdom from his lips Beats a heartless man with a rod. 
10:15: The wise will hide perception, But the mouth of a rash man approaches destruction. 
10:16: The acquisition of riches is a fortified city, But poverty is the destruction of the ungodly. 
10:17: The works of the righteous produce life, But the fruits of the ungodly produce sins. 
10:18: Instruction guards righteous ways of life, But instruction incapable of criticism goes astray. 
10:19: Righteous lips cover hatred, But those who bring forth abuse are very lacking in discernment. 
10:20: You will not escape sin by a multitude of words, But you of discreet lips will be forbearing. 
10:21: The tongue of a righteous man is silver tried in the fire, But the heart of an ungodly man shall fail. 
10:22: The lips of righteous men understand lofty things, But men without discernment die in want. 
10:23: The blessing of the Lord is upon the head of a righteous man; It enriches him, and grief of heart will not be added to it. 
10:24: A man without discernment does evil things with laughter, But wisdom gives birth to discernment in a man. 
10:25: The ungodly man is carried about in destruction, But the desire of the righteous man is acceptable. 
10:26: The ungodly man is destroyed by the passing storm, But the righteous man turns aside, and shall be saved forever. 
10:27: As an unripe grape is harmful to the teeth and smoke to the eyes, So is a lawless woman to the men who use her. 
10:28: The fear of the Lord increases days, But the years of the ungodly will be shortened. 
10:29: Gladness continues for the righteous, But the hope of the ungodly perishes. 
10:30: The fear of the Lord is the stronghold of a holy man, But destruction is for those who work evil. 
10:31: A righteous man will never fail, But the ungodly shall not inhabit the earth. 
10:32: The mouth of a righteous man distills wisdom, But the tongue of an unrighteous man utterly destroys. 
10:33: The lips of righteous men distill grace, But the mouth of the ungodly is perverse.
