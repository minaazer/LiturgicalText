11:1: Deceitful scales are an abomination before the Lord, But a righteous weight is acceptable to Him. 
11:2: Wherever arrogance enters, there also is dishonor, But the mouth of the humble meditates on wisdom. 
11:3: When a righteous man dies, he leaves regret, But the destruction of the ungodly is immediate and brings joy. 
11:4: Righteousness cuts straight and blameless paths, But ungodliness embraces wrongdoing. 
11:5: The righteousness of upright men delivers them, But lawless men are taken to their destruction. 
11:6: When a righteous man dies, his hope does not perish, But the boast of the ungodly perishes. 
11:7: A righteous man escapes from a snare, But the ungodly man is handed over in his place. 
11:8: There is a snare for citizens in the mouth of the ungodly, But the perception of the righteous is prosperous. 
11:9: A city stays upright in the good things of the righteous. But it is razed to the ground by the mouths of the ungodly. 
11:10: A man in need of discernment treats citizens with contempt, But a man of discernment keeps quiet. 
11:11: A double-tongued man reveals deliberations heard in council, But a man faithful in spirit conceals matters. 
11:12: Those for whom there is no leadership fall like leaves, But there is salvation in much counsel. 
11:13: A wicked man does evil, whenever he meets a righteous man, And he hates the sound of stability. 
11:14: A thankful woman raises up glory to her husband, But a throne of dishonor is a woman who hates righteousness. 
11:15: Lazy men are in need of wealth, But courageous men support themselves with wealth. 
11:16: A merciful man does good to his soul, But an unmerciful man utterly destroys his body. 
11:17: An ungodly man does unrighteous works, But the seed of the righteous is a reward of truth. 
11:18: A righteous son is begotten unto life, But the persecution of an ungodly man is unto death. 
11:19: Perverse ways are an abomination to the Lord, But all who are blameless in their ways are acceptable to Him. 
11:20: He who joins hands with the unrighteous will not go unpunished, But he who sows righteousness will receive a faithful reward. 
11:21: Like a ring in the nose of a wild swine, So is beauty to an evil-minded woman. 
11:22: Every desire of the righteous is good, But the hope of the ungodly is destruction. 
11:23: There are those who sow their own things, and produce more things, And there are those who gather, but have less. 
11:24: Every sincere soul is blessed, But an angry man is not graceful. 
11:25: May the one who hoards grain leave it to the nations, But may blessing be upon the head of the one who shares it. 
11:26: He who plans good things seeks good grace, But he who seeks evil, it will capture him. 
11:27: He who trusts in riches, this man will fall, But he who helps the righteous, this man will rise. 
11:28: He who does not deal graciously with his own house will inherit the wind, And a man without discernment will be servant to one with discernment. 
11:29: A tree of life springs up from the fruit of righteousness, But the souls of the lawless will be taken away before their time. 
11:30: If the righteous man is scarcely saved, Then where shall an ungodly man and a sinner appear?
