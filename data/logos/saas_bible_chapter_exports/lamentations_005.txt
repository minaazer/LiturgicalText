5:1: “O Lord, remember what happened to us; Look upon us with favor, And see our disgrace. 
5:2: Our inheritance has been turned over to foreigners, And our homes to strangers. 
5:3: We have become orphans, and have no father; Our mothers are as widows. 
5:4: “We pay for the water we drink, And our wood comes at a price. 
5:5: We are pursued at our heels; We have labored; We have no rest. 
5:6: Egypt gave us a hand, Assyria to their satisfaction. 
5:7: “Our fathers sinned, and are no more; We suffered from their lawless actions. 
5:8: Servants rule over us; There is no one to redeem us from their hand. 
5:9: We will bring in our bread At the risk of our lives, From the presence of the sword in the desert. 
5:10: “Our skin has became blackened as an oven. They are shriveled up from the stormy wind of famine. 
5:11: They humbled the women in Zion, The virgins in the cities of Judah. 
5:12: Princes were hung up by their hands, And elders were not honored. 
5:13: The chosen men took up weeping, And the young men weakened under the loads of wood. 
5:14: Elders also ceased to gather at the gate; The chosen men ceased to sing their psalms. 
5:15: “The joy of our heart has ceased; Our dance has turned into mourning. 
5:16: The crown fell from our head; Woe to us, because we sinned! 
5:17: Because of this, our heart has become painful, Because of this, our eyes have remained in darkness. 
5:18: On Mount Zion, because it is desolate, Foxes pass through it. 
5:19: “But You, O Lord, shall dwell forever; Your throne from generation to generation. 
5:20: Why will You utterly forget us? Will You forsake us for so long a time? 
5:21: Turn us back to You, O Lord, And we shall be converted; Renew our days as before. 
5:22: For You have indeed rejected us, And are exceedingly angry with us.”
