16:1: So they brought in the ark of God and set it in the midst of the tent David had pitched for it. They offered up whole burnt offerings and peace offerings before God. 
16:2: And David finished offering the whole burnt offerings and peace offerings, and blessed the people in the name of the Lord. 
16:3: And he divided one loaf of baked bread to every man and woman of Israel. 
16:4: And he appointed Levites to minister before the presence of the ark of the Lord, to exclaim and give thanks and to praise the Lord God of Israel: 
16:5: Asaph the chief, and next to him Zechariah, then Jeiel, Shemiramoth, Jehiel, Mattithiah, Eliab, Benaiah, and Obed-Edom; Jeiel with stringed instruments and harps, but Asaph made music with cymbals; 
16:6: Benaiah and Jahaziel the priests, continually before the ark of the covenant of God with trumpets. 
16:7: Then David by the hand of Asaph and his brethren gave orders to praise the Lord: 
16:8: Give praise to the Lord Himself! Call upon His name; Make known His deeds among the peoples. 
16:9: Sing songs to Him, sing praises to Him; Tell of all His wondrous works which the Lord has done. 
16:10: Praise His holy name; The heart that seeks to do His pleasure shall rejoice. 
16:11: Seek the Lord and be strong; On account of all things, seek His face. 
16:12: Remember His remarkable works which He has done, His wonders and the judgments of His mouth, 
16:13: O seed of Israel His servant, You children of Jacob, His chosen ones. 
16:14: He is the Lord our Lord; His judgments are over all the earth. 
16:15: Let us remember His covenant unto the ages, The word He commands for a thousand generations— 
16:16: The covenant He made with Abraham And His oath to Isaac; 
16:17: He confirmed it to Jacob for an ordinance, To Israel for an eternal covenant, 
16:18: Saying, “To you I will give the land of Canaan As the allotment of your inheritance.” 
16:19: When they were few in number, When they were small in stature and dwelt as sojourners in it, 
16:20: And when they went from nation to nation And from one kingdom to another people, 
16:21: He allowed no man to rule over them, And He reproved kings for their sakes, 
16:22: Saying, “Do not touch My anointed ones, And bring no harm to My prophets.” 
16:23: Sing to the Lord, all the earth; Proclaim His salvation from day to day. 
16:24: For the Lord is great and greatly to be praised; He is to be feared above all gods. 
16:25: For all the gods of the Gentiles are idols, But our God made the heavens. 
16:26: Glory and praise are before Him; Strength and rejoicing are in His place. 
16:27: Give to the Lord, O families of the peoples, Give to the Lord glory and strength. 
16:28: Give to the Lord the glory due His name; Take gifts and offer them before His presence. Oh, worship the Lord in His holy courts! 
16:29: Let all the earth fear because of His presence; Let the earth be set upright And not be moved. 
16:30: Let the heavens rejoice, and let the earth be glad; And let them say among the nations, “The Lord reigns.” 
16:31: Let the sea roar and all its fullness, And the trees of the forest and all that is in them. 
16:32: For the trees of the forest shall rejoice before the Lord, For He is coming to judge the earth. 
16:33: Oh, give thanks to the Lord, for He is good, For His mercy endures forever. 
16:34: And say, “Save us, O God of our salvation; And deliver us from the Gentiles To praise Your holy name, And boast in Your praises.” 
16:35: Blessed be the Lord God of Israel From ages unto the ages. All the people said, “Amen.” And they praised the Lord. 
16:36: And they left Asaph and his brothers there, before the ark of the covenant of the Lord, to minister continually from day to day according to the service of each day. 
16:37: And Obed-Edom and his brothers were sixty-eight; Obed-Edom the son of Jeduthun, and Hosah were gatekeepers; 
16:38: Zadok the priest and the brethren of the priests stood before the tabernacle of the Lord in the high place in Gibeon, 
16:39: to offer whole burnt offerings to the Lord on the altar of whole burnt offering every morning and evening; and to do according to all things written in the Law of the Lord that He commanded the sons of Israel by the hand of Moses the servant of God. 
16:40: And with him were Heman and Jeduthun, and the rest chosen out by name to give thanks to the Lord, for His mercy is forever; 
16:41: and with them Heman and Jeduthun, to sound aloud with trumpets and cymbals and the musical instruments of God. Now the sons of Jeduthun were gatekeepers. 
16:42: Then all the people departed, every man to his house; and David returned to bless his house.
