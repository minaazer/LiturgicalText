4:1: For the End; in Psalms; An Ode by David. 
4:2: You heard me when I called, O God of my righteousness. You strengthened my heart when I was in distress; Have mercy on me, and hear my prayer. 
4:3: How long, O you sons of men, Will you be slow of heart? Why do you love vain things, And seek after lies? 
4:4: Know that the Lord made His Holy One wondrous; The Lord will hear me when I cry to Him. 
4:5: Be angry, and do not sin; Have remorse upon your beds For what you say in your hearts. (Pause) 
4:6: Offer the sacrifice of righteousness, And hope in the Lord. 
4:7: There are many who say, “Who will show us good things?” O Lord, the light of your face was stamped upon us. 
4:8: You put gladness in my heart; From their season of wheat, wine and oil they were multiplied. 
4:9: I will both sleep and rest in peace, For you alone, O Lord, cause me to dwell in hope.
