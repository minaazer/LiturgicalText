19:1: Then Job answered and said: 
19:2: “How long will you weary my soul, And take me down with words? 
19:3: Only know how the Lord has dealt with me thus. You speak against me, and while shaming me, You press hard on me. 
19:4: Yes indeed I have truly erred; my error lodges with me. When I speak words, which I should not have spoken, My words go astray and are unseasonable. 
19:5: Alas, you exalt yourselves against me, And attack me with disgrace. 
19:6: Know therefore, that the Lord has troubled me, And raised His stronghold against me. 
19:7: Behold, I laugh at disgrace and I will not speak. Nor I will cry out, for judgment is nowhere to be found. 
19:8: I am walled in round about, and cannot cross over, For He has set darkness before my face. 
19:9: He stripped me of my glory, And took the crown from my head. 
19:10: He has torn me apart on every side, and I am gone. He has uprooted my hope like a tree. 
19:11: He handled me vehemently in wrath, And has counted me like an enemy. 
19:12: His raiders came against me with one accord. They encircled my paths, and lay in wait. 
19:13: “My brothers stood far from me, And recognized strangers rather than me. So my friends became unmerciful. 
19:14: My relatives acted as though they did not know me, And my close friends have forgotten me. 
19:15: As for my neighbors and the maidservants of my house, I was a stranger before them. 
19:16: I called my servant, but he gave no answer, Though I begged him with my mouth. 
19:17: I also entreated my wife, And summoned the children of my concubines, and flattered them. 
19:18: But they rejected me forever. When I arise, they speak against me. 
19:19: Those who know me detest me, And those whom I truly love turned against me. 
19:20: The muscles of my body have decayed under my skin, And my bones are held in my teeth. 
19:21: “Have mercy on me, have mercy on me, O friends, For the hand of the Lord has touched me. 
19:22: Why do you persecute me as the Lord does, And are not satisfied of my flesh? 
19:23: “For who may commit my words to writing, To put them in a book forever, 
19:24: With an iron pen and lead, Or to engrave them in rocks? 
19:25: For I know He is everlasting, He who is about to set me free on the earth, 
19:26: And to raise up my skin that endures these things; For these have been accomplished for me by the Lord; 
19:27: Which I myself am conscious of, And my eyes have beheld, and not those of another. All has been accomplished for me in my bosom. 
19:28: If you shall say, ‘What shall we say before him? Then we will find in him the root of the matter.’ 
19:29: Indeed, be afraid to cover up your evil, For wrath will come upon lawless men; Then they will know where their substance is.”
