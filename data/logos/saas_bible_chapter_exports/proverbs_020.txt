20:1: Wine is an intemperate thing, and strong drink is full of violence; And all who commingle with it will not be wise. 
20:2: The threat of a king does not differ from the anger of a lion, And he who provokes him sins against his own soul. 
20:3: It is glory for a man to turn away from reproaches, But every man without discernment is entangled with such things. 
20:4: A lazy man who is reproached feels no shame, Likewise also is he who borrows bread in harvest. 
20:5: Counsel is deep water in the heart of a man, And a man with discernment will draw it out. 
20:6: Man is important, and a man who shows mercy is noble, But it is difficult to find a faithful man. 
20:7: He who lives blamelessly in righteousness, He will leave blessed children behind him. 
20:8: When a righteous king sits upon the throne, No evil thing can oppose his presence. 
20:9: Who will boast about having a pure heart? Or who will declare boldly being clean from sins? 
20:10: The lamp of him who reviles his father or mother shall be put out, And the pupils of his eyes shall see darkness. 
20:11: A portion gained hastily in the beginning, Will not be blessed at the end. 
20:12: Do not say, “I will avenge myself on the enemy,” But wait on the Lord, that you may be helped. 
20:13: A weight, great and small, and a double standard, Both are also unclean before the Lord. 
20:14: And the one who uses these things in his pursuits will be entangled; But a young man with holiness, his road is straight. 
20:15: The ear hears and the eye sees; Both are also works of the Lord. 
20:16: Do not love to speak ill, That you may not be taken away; 
20:17: Open your eyes, And you will be filled with bread. 
20:18: A double standard is an abomination to the Lord, And a deceitful scale is not good before Him. 
20:19: Steps are made straight for a man by the Lord, But how can a mortal man understand His ways? 
20:20: It is a snare for a man to quickly sanctify something of his own things as holy, Then after making this vow, to come to a change of heart. 
20:21: A wise king is a winnower of the ungodly, And he casts them upon the wheel of torture. 
20:22: The breath of man is the light of the Lord, Who searches the inner man. 
20:23: Mercy and truth are a guard for the king, And they will encircle his throne with righteousness. 
20:24: Wisdom is an ornament to young men, And the glory of old men is their gray hair. 
20:25: Bruises and fractures happen to evil men, And plagues shall come into their inner man.
