52:1: For the End; for Mahaleth; Concerning Understanding; by David. 
52:2: The fool said in his heart, “There is no God.” They are corrupt and abominable in lawlessness; There is none who does good. 
52:3: God looked from heaven upon the sons of men To see if there were any who understood or sought God. 
52:4: All turned aside; they were altogether rendered useless; There is none who does good, not even one. 
52:5: Do all the workers of lawlessness not know? They eat up my people as they eat bread, and do not call upon God. 
52:6: They were in great fear where no fear was, For God scattered the bones of men-pleasers; They were put to shame, because God scorned them. 
52:7: Who will bring the salvation of Israel from Zion? When God brings back the captivity of His people, Jacob will greatly rejoice, and Israel will be glad.
