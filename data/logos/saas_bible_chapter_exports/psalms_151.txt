151:1: I was small among my brothers, And the youngest in my father’s house; I tended my father’s sheep.
151:2: My hands built a musical instrument; My fingers tuned a lyre.
151:3: And who shall tell my Lord? The Lord Himself, He Himself hears.
151:4: He sent forth His angel, And took me from my father’s sheep; And he anointed me with his anointing oil.
151:5: My brothers were handsome and tall, But the Lord took no pleasure in them.
151:6: I went out to meet the foreigner; And he cursed me with his idols;
151:7: But I drew his own sword, And beheaded him, And removed disgrace from the children of Israel.
