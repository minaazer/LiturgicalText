13:1: “Behold, my eye has seen all these things, And my ear has heard them. 
13:2: What you know, I also know; I am not of lesser understanding than you. 
13:3: Nevertheless I will speak to the Lord, And will reason before Him if He wills. 
13:4: For you are all dishonest physicians and healers of diseases. 
13:5: But would that you were silent, For it would lead you to wisdom. 
13:6: Hear the conviction of my mouth, And heed the judgment of my lips. 
13:7: Do you not speak before the Lord, And utter deceit before Him? 
13:8: Will you draw back? But you yourselves be judges. 
13:9: It would be well for you if He searches you out. For if you make every effort to join yourselves to Him, 
13:10: He will surely rebuke you if you also secretly show favoritism. 
13:11: Will not His terror make you dizzy, And His fear fall upon you? 
13:12: In the end, your insolence will go away like ashes, And your body like clay. 
13:13: “Be silent, that I may speak, And put my anger to rest, 
13:14: So I may take my flesh in my teeth, And put my life in my hand. 
13:15: Though the Mighty One should lay His hand upon me, And already He has begun, I will speak and reason before Him. 
13:16: This will turn for me into salvation, For there shall be no deceit before Him. 
13:17: Listen, listen to my words, For I will report them in your hearing. 
13:18: Behold, I am near my judgment: I know I shall appear righteous. 
13:19: Who is he who will judge me, That I should now be silent and be wanting? 
13:20: Grant me two things, Then I will not hide my face from You: 
13:21: Withdraw Your hand far from me, And let not the fear of You terrify me. 
13:22: Then You will call, and I will obey You; Or You will speak, then I will give You an answer. 
13:23: How many are my sins and transgressions? Teach me what they are. 
13:24: Why do You hide Yourself from me, And regard me as Your enemy? 
13:25: Will You take heed of me, as a leaf shaken by the wind? Or will You set Yourself against me, as grass blown by the wind? 
13:26: For You catalogued evil things against me, And compassed me with the sins of my youth. 
13:27: You have put my foot in the stocks, Have watched all my works, And have penetrated into the roots of my feet, 
13:28: Which have become old like a wineskin Or like a moth-eaten garment.
