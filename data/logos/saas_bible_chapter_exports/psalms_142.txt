142:1: A Psalm by David, When His Son Persecuted Him. O Lord, hear my prayer, Give ear to my supplication In Your truth; Answer me in Your righteousness; 
142:2: Do not enter into judgment with Your servant, For no one living shall be righteous in Your sight. 
142:3: For the enemy persecuted my soul; He humbled my life to the ground; He caused me to dwell in dark places as one long dead; 
142:4: And my spirit was in anguish within me; My heart was troubled within me. 
142:5: I remembered the days of old, And I meditated on all Your works; I meditated on the works of Your hands. 
142:6: I spread out my hands to You; My soul thirsts for You like a waterless land. (Pause) 
142:7: Hear me speedily, O Lord; My spirit faints within me; Turn not Your face from me, Lest I become like those who go down into the pit. 
142:8: Cause me to hear Your mercy in the morning, For I hope in You; Make me know, O Lord, the way wherein I should walk, For I lift up my soul up to You. 
142:9: Deliver me from my enemies, O Lord, For to You I flee for refuge. 
142:10: Teach me to do Your will, For You are my God; Your good Spirit shall guide me in the land of uprightness. 
142:11: For Your name’s sake, O Lord, give me life; In Your righteousness You shall bring my soul out of affliction. 
142:12: In Your mercy You shall destroy my enemies; You shall utterly destroy all who afflict my soul, For I am Your servant.
