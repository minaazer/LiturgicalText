121:1: An Ode of Ascents. I was glad when they said to me, “Let us go into the house of the Lord.” 
121:2: Our feet stand In your courts, O Jerusalem. 
121:3: Jerusalem is built as a city, Whose compactness is complete. 
121:4: There the tribes went up, The tribes of the Lord, a testimony to Israel, To give thanks to the name of the Lord. 
121:5: For thrones sat there for judgment, Thrones over the house of David. 
121:6: Ask now for things regarding the peace of Jerusalem, And there is prosperity for those who love you; 
121:7: Let there now be peace in your power, And prosperity in your citadels. 
121:8: For the sake of my brothers and my neighbors, I indeed spoke peace concerning you; 
121:9: For the sake of the house of the Lord our God, I sought good things for you.
