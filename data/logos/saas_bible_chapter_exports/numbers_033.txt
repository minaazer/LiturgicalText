33:1: Now these are the stages in the journeys of the children of Israel, who went out from the land of Egypt by their armies under the hand of Moses and Aaron. 
33:2: So Moses wrote their departures and the stages in their journeys by the word of the Lord. 
33:3: They departed from Rameses in the first month, on the fifteenth day of the first month; on the day after Pascha, the children of Israel went out with a high hand before all the Egyptians. 
33:4: But the Egyptians were burying from among themselves all who died, whom the Lord struck down, every firstborn in the land of Egypt; and the Lord executed vengeance on their gods. 
33:5: Then the children of Israel departed from Rameses and camped in Succoth. 
33:6: They departed from Succoth and camped in Etham, which is part of the desert. 
33:7: They departed from Etham and camped at the mouth of Hahiroth, which is opposite Baal Zephon; and they camped opposite Migdol. 
33:8: They departed opposite Hahiroth, and passed through the midst of the Sea into the desert; and they went three days’ journey through the desert, and camped at Marah. 
33:9: They departed from Marah and came to Elim. At Elim there were twelve springs of water and seventy palm trees; so they camped there by the water. 
33:10: They departed from Elim and camped by the Red Sea. 
33:11: They departed from the Red Sea and camped in the Desert of Sin. 
33:12: They departed from the Desert of Sin and camped at Dophkah. 
33:13: They departed from Dophkah and camped at Alush. 
33:14: They departed from Alush and camped at Rephidim, where there was no water for the people to drink. 
33:15: They departed from Rephidim and camped in the Sinai Desert. 
33:16: They moved from the Sinai Desert and camped at Graves of Lust. 
33:17: They departed from Graves of Lust and camped at Hazeroth. 
33:18: They departed from Hazeroth and camped at Rithmah. 
33:19: They departed from Rithmah and camped at Rimmon Perez. 
33:20: They departed from Rimmon Perez and camped at Libnah. 
33:21: They departed from Libnah and camped at Rissah. 
33:22: They departed from Rissah and camped at Kehelathah. 
33:23: They departed from Kehelathah and camped at Shepher. 
33:24: They departed from Shepher and camped at Haradah. 
33:25: They departed from Haradah and camped at Makheloth. 
33:26: They departed from Makheloth and camped at Tahath. 
33:27: They departed from Tahath and camped at Terah. 
33:28: They departed from Terah and camped at Mithkah. 
33:29: They departed from Mithkah and camped at Hashmonah. 
33:30: They departed from Hashmonah and camped at Moseroth. 
33:31: They departed from Moseroth and camped at Bene Jaakan. 
33:32: They departed from Bene Jaakan and camped at Hor Hagidgad. 
33:33: They departed from Hor Hagidgad and camped at Jotbathah. 
33:34: They departed from Jotbathah and camped at Abronah. 
33:35: They departed from Abronah and camped at Ezion Geber. 
33:36: They departed from Ezion Geber and camped in the Desert of Sin; and they departed from the Desert of Sin and camped in the Desert of Paran (this is Kadesh). 
33:37: They departed from Kadesh and camped at Mount Hor, near the land of Edom. 
33:38: Then Aaron the priest went up at the command of the Lord, and died there in the fortieth year of the exodus of the children of Israel out of the land of Egypt, in the fifth month, on the first day of the month. 
33:39: Aaron was one hundred and twenty-three years old when he died on Mount Hor. 
33:40: Now this was the time when the Canaanite king of Arad (and this man dwelt in the land of Canaan) heard the children of Israel were coming. 
33:41: Then they departed from Mount Hor and camped at Zalmonah. 
33:42: They departed from Zalmonah and camped at Punon. 
33:43: They departed from Punon and camped at Oboth. 
33:44: They departed from Oboth and camped at Ije Abarim, at the borders of Moab. 
33:45: They departed from Ije Abarim and camped at Dibon Gad. 
33:46: They departed from Dibon Gad and camped at Almon Diblathaim. 
33:47: They departed from Almon Diblathaim and camped in the mountains of Abarim, before Nebo. 
33:48: They departed from the mountains of Abarim and camped west of Moab by the Jordan opposite Jericho. 
33:49: They camped by the Jordan between Beth Jesimoth as far as the Abel Acacia Grove, west of Moab. 
33:50: Now the Lord spoke to Moses west of Moab by the Jordan opposite Jericho, saying, 
33:51: “Speak to the children of Israel, and say to them, ‘You are crossing over the Jordan into the land of Canaan. 
33:52: Then you shall destroy all the inhabitants of the land before your face; and you shall remove their high places, and as for all their molten idols, you shall destroy these; and you shall remove all their pillars. 
33:53: You shall also destroy all the inhabitants of the land and dwell in it, for I give you their land as an inheritance. 
33:54: Therefore you shall inherit their land by lot among your tribes; to the larger you shall give a larger inheritance, and to the smaller you shall give a smaller inheritance; there everyone’s inheritance shall be whatever falls to him by lot. You shall inherit according to the tribes of your fathers. 
33:55: But if you do not destroy the inhabitants of the land from before your face, it shall be that those whom you let remain shall be thorn in your eyes and arrows in your sides; and they shall be at enmity with you in the land where you dwell. 
33:56: Moreover it shall be that I will do to you as I thought to do to them.’ ”
