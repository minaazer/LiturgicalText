150:1: Alleluia. Praise God in His saints; Praise Him in the firmament of His power; 
150:2: Praise Him for His mighty acts; Praise Him according to the abundance of His greatness. 
150:3: Praise Him with the sound of a trumpet; Praise Him with the harp and lyre; 
150:4: Praise Him with timbrel and dance; Praise Him with strings and flute; 
150:5: Praise Him with resounding cymbals; Praise Him with triumphant cymbals, 
150:6: Let everything that breathes praise the Lord. Alleluia.
