24:1: Wisdom will praise herself, And in the midst of her people she will boast. 
24:2: In the assembly of the Most High, she will open her mouth, And boast in the presence of His host. 
24:3: “I came forth from the mouth of the Most High, And covered the earth like a mist. 
24:4: I pitched my tent in the high places, And my throne on the pillar of cloud. 
24:5: I alone encircle the ring of heaven, And I walk in the depth of the abyss. 
24:6: In the waves of the sea, in all the earth, And in every people and nation I have gained a possession. 
24:7: With all these, I have sought a place to rest; In what inheritance will I lodge? 
24:8: “Then the Creator of all things commanded me, And He who created me gave me a place to live. He said, ‘Pitch your tent in Jacob And receive an inheritance in Israel.’ 
24:9: Before the age, He created me from the beginning, And in the age to come I will not cease. 
24:10: I serve before Him in His holy tabernacle; Therefore, I am established in Zion. 
24:11: In the beloved city likewise He gives me rest, And my authority is in Jerusalem. 
24:12: I am firmly rooted among an honored people, Among the Lord’s portion, His inheritance. 
24:13: “Like a cedar tree I was raised on high in Lebanon; Like a cypress tree in the mountains of Hermon. 
24:14: I was exalted like a palm tree in En-Geddi, And like rose bushes in Jericho; I was exalted like a beautiful olive tree in the plain, And grew tall like a plane-tree. 
24:15: I gave forth the fragrance of aromatic spices, Like cinnamon and camel-thorn, And I spread a fragrance like choice myrrh, And like galbanum, onyx and oil of myrrh, And like the fragrance of frankincense in the tabernacle. 
24:16: I spread forth my branches like a terebinth tree, And my branches were glory and grace. 
24:17: I yield grace like a vine, And my flowers produce the fruit of glory and riches. 
24:18: I am the mother of fair love, and fear, and knowledge, and holy hope: I therefore, being eternal, am given to all my children who are named by Him. 
24:19: “Come to me, you who desire me, And take your fill of my fruits. 
24:20: For the remembrance of me is sweeter than honey, And my inheritance is sweeter than the honeycomb. 
24:21: Those who eat me will hunger for more, And those who drink me will thirst for more. 
24:22: He who obeys me will not be put to shame, And those who work with me will not sin.” 
24:23: All this is the book of the covenant of the Most High God; The law Moses commanded us, The inheritance of the congregations of Jacob. 
24:24: Faint not to be strong in the Lord; That He may confirm you, cleave unto Him: For the Lord Almighty is God alone, And beside Him there is no other Savior. 
24:25: This law fills men with wisdom, Like the Pishon and the Tigris at the time of first fruits; 
24:26: And it fills them with understanding, Like the Euphrates and the Jordan at the time of harvest. 
24:27: This law shines forth instruction like light, Like the Gihon in the days of vintage. 
24:28: As the first man did not know wisdom perfectly, So the last man has not searched out her depths. 
24:29: For her thoughts are deeper than the sea, And her counsel than the great abyss. 
24:30: I went forth like a canal from a river, And like an aqueduct into a garden. 
24:31: I said, “I will water my garden, And drench the garden beds.” And behold, my canal became a river, And my river became a sea. 
24:32: I will yet make instruction shine like the morning, And I will make it shine far away. 
24:33: I will yet pour forth teaching like prophecy, And I will leave it behind for future generations. 
24:34: As you can see, I have not labored for myself alone, But for all who seek wisdom.
