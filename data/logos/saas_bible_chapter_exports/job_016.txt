16:1: Then Job answered and said: 
16:2: “I have heard many such things; You are all bad comforters. 
16:3: What order is there in words of wind? Or what troubles you, that you answer thus? 
16:4: I also could speak as you do, If indeed your soul were in my soul’s place. Then I would attack you with words, And shake my head at you. 
16:5: But would that there were strength in my mouth: Then I would not spare the movement of my lips. 
16:6: “If I should speak, I would not feel the pain of my wound, But if I also should be silent, how would I be less wounded? 
16:7: But now He has made me weary and a worn-out fool, And you have laid hold of me. 
16:8: My falsehood has become a testimony. It rises up, and argues against me to my face. 
16:9: He vented His wrath, and struck me down; He gnashed at me with His teeth; The arrows of the raiders fell on me. 
16:10: He attacked me with the sharp darts of His eyes; He struck me on the cheek; With one accord they ran me down. 
16:11: For the Lord delivered me into the hands of unjust men, And cast me upon the ungodly. 
16:12: When I was at peace, He rejected me. He took me by the hair of my head, and pulled it out. He set me up as a mark. 
16:13: They surrounded me with spears aimed at my loins. Without sparing me, they poured out my gall on the ground. 
16:14: Running at me with all their strength, They knocked me down, head over heels. 
16:15: They sewed sackcloth on my skin, And my strength was spent on the ground. 
16:16: My belly is on fire because of weeping, And shade is on my eyelids. 
16:17: Yet there was no wrongdoing in my hands, And my prayer is pure. 
16:18: O earth, do not cover the blood of my flesh, Nor may my cry have a place. 
16:19: Now behold, my witness is in the heavens, And the One who knows me thoroughly is on high. 
16:20: May my supplication come before the Lord, And may my eyes pour out tears in His presence. 
16:21: May there be rebuttal for a man before the Lord, As a son of man with his neighbor. 
16:22: For my years are numbered, And I shall go the way of no return.
