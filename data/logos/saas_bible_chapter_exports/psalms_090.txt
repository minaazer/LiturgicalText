90:1: The Praise of an Ode by David. He who dwells in the help of the Most High Shall lodge in the shelter of the God of heaven. 
90:2: He shall say to the Lord, “You are my protector and my refuge, My God; I will hope in Him; 
90:3: For He shall save me from the snare of the hunters, And from every troubling word. 
90:4: He shall overshadow you with His shoulders, And under His wings you shall hope; His truth shall encircle you with a shield. 
90:5: You shall not be frightened by fear at night, Nor from an arrow that flies by day, 
90:6: Nor by a thing moving in darkness, Nor by mishap and a demon of noonday. 
90:7: A thousand shall fall at your side, And ten thousand at your right hand, Yet it shall not come near you; 
90:8: But you shall observe with your eyes, And you shall see the reward of sinners. 
90:9: For You, O Lord, are my hope; You made the Most High your refuge. 
90:10: Evils shall not come to you, And a scourge shall not draw near your dwelling; 
90:11: For He shall command His angels concerning you, To keep you in all your ways; 
90:12: In their hands they shall bear you up, Lest you strike your foot against a stone; 
90:13: You shall tread upon the asp and the basilisk, And you shall trample the lion and the dragon. 
90:14: For he hoped in Me, and I will deliver him; I will shelter him, because he knew My name. 
90:15: He shall call upon Me, and I will hear him; I am with him in affliction, And I will deliver and glorify him. 
90:16: With length of days I will satisfy him, And show him My salvation.
