14:1: “For mortal man, born of woman, is short-lived and full of wrath. 
14:2: He falls like a flower that blooms, And like a shadow, he does not continue. 
14:3: Have You not taken account of him, And brought him to judgment before You? 
14:4: For who shall be pure from uncleanness? No one. 
14:5: Even if his life is but one day upon the earth, His months are numbered by You. You appointed a time for him, and he cannot exceed it. 
14:6: Depart from him that he may rest, And let him be pleased with his life as a hired laborer. 
14:7: “For there is hope for a tree, even if it is cut down, That it will sprout again, and its tender shoots will not fail. 
14:8: For though its root may grow old in the earth, And its stump may die in the rocky ground, 
14:9: Yet at the scent of water it will bud, And bring forth a crop like one newly planted. 
14:10: But a man who dies is utterly gone, And when a mortal has fallen, he is no more. 
14:11: For in time the sea is drawn down, And a river stops flowing; And the river bed dries up. 
14:12: So too, when man falls asleep in death he will not rise, For the dead will not awake until the sky is stitched together. 
14:13: “For would that You had kept me in the grave, That You had hidden me until Your wrath ceases, And that You would appoint me a set time In which you would remember me. 
14:14: For will man live again After he completes his course of life and dies? I will wait until I arise. 
14:15: Then You will call, and I will answer You, But do not cast away the works of Your hands. 
14:16: For You have numbered my practices, And not one of my sins will escape You. 
14:17: You sealed up my lawlessness in a bag, And marked if I transgressed unwillingly. 
14:18: “Truly a mountain falls, and crumbles in pieces; And a rock wears out in its place. 
14:19: Waters wear away stones, And overflowing torrents wash away the soil of the earth; And You destroy the duration of man. 
14:20: You push him to his end, and he is gone. You set your face against him, and send him away. 
14:21: If his children multiply themselves, he does not know it, And if they be few, he does not perceive it. 
14:22: But the muscles of his body feel pain, And his soul grieves.”
