35:1: Again the word of the Lord came to me, saying, 
35:2: “Son of man, set your face against Mount Seir and prophesy against it, 
35:3: and say to it, ‘Thus says the Lord and Master: “Behold, O Mount Seir, I am against you, and I will stretch out My hand against you. I will cause you to become a desert, and you will be wasted. 
35:4: I will also cause desolation in your cities, and you shall be desolate. Then you will know that I am the Lord. 
35:5: Because you have an eternal hatred for the house of Israel, and laid in wait deceitfully with sword in hand as enemies, in a time of wrongdoing to the extreme; 
35:6: therefore, as I live,” says the Lord and Master, “truly you sinned even to blood, thus blood shall pursue you. 
35:7: I will cause Mount Seir to become a desert and a desolate place, and destroy man and cattle from it. 
35:8: I will fill your hills and valleys with your slain, and in all your plains and among you the slain shall fall by the sword. 
35:9: I will make you an eternal desolation, and your cities will be inhabited no more. Then you will know that I am the Lord. 
35:10: “Because you said, ‘The two nations and the two countries shall be mine, and I shall inherit them,’ though the Lord is there; 
35:11: therefore, as I live,” says the Lord, “I will deal with you according to your enmity, and I will be made known to you when I judge you. 
35:12: Then you will know that I am the Lord. I heard the voice of your blasphemies when you said, ‘The desert mountains of Israel are given to us as food.’ 
35:13: You boasted against Me with your mouth, and I heard it.” 
35:14: Thus says the Lord: “When all the earth is in gladness, I will make you a desert. 
35:15: You will be a desert, O Mount Seir, and all of Edom will be utterly destroyed. Then you will know that I am the Lord their God.’ ”
