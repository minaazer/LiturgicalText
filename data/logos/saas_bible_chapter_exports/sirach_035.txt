35:1: The man who keeps the law will abound in offerings; He who heeds the commandments sacrifices a peace offering. 
35:2: He who returns a kindness offers fine wheat flour, And he who does alms sacrifices a praise offering. 
35:3: To forsake evil is pleasing to the Lord, And to forsake wrongdoing is atonement. 
35:4: Do not appear before the Lord empty-handed, For all these offerings are made because of the commandment. 
35:5: A righteous offering anoints the altar, And its fragrance rises before the Most High. 
35:6: The sacrifice of a righteous man is acceptable, And its remembrance shall not be forgotten. 
35:7: Glorify the Lord with your generosity, And do not reduce the first fruits of your hands. 
35:8: In every gift let your face be cheerful, And sanctify your tithe with gladness. 
35:9: Give to the Most High as Hehas given to you, And give to Him with generosity, according to your windfall. 
35:10: For the Lord is He who repays, And He will repay you sevenfold. 
35:11: Do not bribe Him, for He will not accept it; And do not offer an unrighteous sacrifice. 
35:12: For the Lord is the judge, And there is no partiality with Him. 
35:13: He will not show partiality to a poor man, But He will hear the petition of him who is wronged. 
35:14: He will not overlook the supplication of an orphan, Nor a widow when she pours out her story. 
35:15: Do not the tears of a widow run down her cheeks, As she cries out against him who caused them? 
35:16: He whose service is pleasing to the Lord, Will be accepted by Him, And his prayer will reach to the clouds. 
35:17: The prayer of a humble man passes through the clouds, And he will not be comforted until it reaches the Lord; 
35:18: And he will not withdraw it until the Most High visits him, Provides justice for the righteous, And executes judgment. 
35:19: So the Lord will not delay, Neither will He be slow to help them, 
35:20: Until He crushes the loins of the unmerciful, And repays vengeance upon the nations, 
35:21: Until He destroys the multitude of the insolent, And crushes the scepter of the unrighteous, 
35:22: Until He renders to a man according to his practices, And the works of men according to their devices, 
35:23: Until He judges the cause of His people, And brings them joy with His mercy. 
35:24: Mercy is beautiful in the time of His affliction, Like rain in the time of drought.
