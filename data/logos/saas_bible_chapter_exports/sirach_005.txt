5:1: Do not set your heart on your possessions, And do not say, “I am independent.” 
5:2: Do not follow yourself and your strength, So as to walk in the desires of your heart; 
5:3: And do not say, “Who will be lord over me?” For the Lord will surely punish you. 
5:4: Do not say, “I sinned, so what happened to me?” For the Lord is patient. 
5:5: Do not be so confident of atonement That you add sin to sin; 
5:6: And do not say, “His compassion is great; He will atone for the multitude of my sins,” For both mercy and wrath come from Him; And His anger rests on sinners. 
5:7: Do not delay to turn to the Lord, And do not put it off from day to day; For suddenly the wrath of the Lord will come forth, And in the day of vengeance, you will perish. 
5:8: Do not set your heart on dishonest wealth, For it will profit you nothing in the day of distress. 
5:9: Do not winnow with every wind, And do not follow every road; For a double-tongued sinner is of such a kind. 
5:10: Be established in your understanding, And let your word be consistent. 
5:11: Be quick to listen, And give your answer with patience. 
5:12: If you have understanding, answer your neighbor, But if not, let your hand be over your mouth. 
5:13: There is glory and dishonor in speech, And a man’s tongue may cause him to fall. 
5:14: Do not be called a slanderer, And do not lie in ambush with your tongue; For shame awaits a thief, And a grievous condemnation will come upon A double-tongued man. 
5:15: In a great or in a small matter, do not go wrong, And do not be an enemy instead of a friend; For a bad name brings shame and disgrace, And a double-tongued sinner is of such a kind.
