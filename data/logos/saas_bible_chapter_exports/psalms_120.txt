120:1: An Ode of Ascents. I lifted my eyes to the mountains; From where shall my help come? 
120:2: My help comes from the Lord, Who made heaven and earth. 
120:3: Let not your foot be moved; Neither let Him who keeps you slumber. 
120:4: Behold, He who keeps Israel Shall not slumber nor sleep. 
120:5: The Lord shall keep you; The Lord is your shelter at your right hand. 
120:6: The sun shall not burn you up by day, Nor the moon by night. 
120:7: The Lord shall keep you from all evil; The Lord shall keep your soul. 
120:8: The Lord shall keep your coming in and your going out From this present time and unto the ages.
