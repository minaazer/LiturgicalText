28:1: An ungodly man flees when no one is pursuing, But a righteous man is confident as a lion. 
28:2: Condemnations arise because of the sins of the ungodly, But an astute man quenches them. 
28:3: A bold man harasses the poor with ungodliness. As a driving and unprofitable rain, 
28:4: So those who forsake the law praise ungodliness, But those who love the law surround themselves with a wall. 
28:5: Evil men do not understand judgment, But those who seek the Lord shall understand it in everything. 
28:6: A poor man walking in the truth is better than a rich liar. 
28:7: A wise son keeps the law, But he who cherishes luxury, dishonors his father. 
28:8: He who increases his wealth with usury and unjust gains, Gathers it for him who shows mercy to the poor. 
28:9: He who turns away his ear so as not to hear the law, He also makes his prayer repulsive. 
28:10: He who leads upright men astray in an evil way, He shall fall into destruction; 
28:11: And lawless men shall pass by good things, But they shall not enter into them. 
28:12: A rich man is wise in his own eyes, But a poor man shall disapprove of his thoughts. 
28:13: Great glory arises because of the help of the righteous, But in places of the ungodly, men are conquered. 
28:14: He who hides his own ungodliness shall not prosper, But he who leads his own rebuttal shall be loved. 
28:15: Blessed is the man who shall be discreet in all things because of godly fear, But the man who hardens his heart shall fall into evils. 
28:16: A hungry lion and a thirsty wolf Is he who being poor, tyrannizes over poor people. 
28:17: A king in need of revenues is a great oppressor, But he who hates unrighteousness shall live a long time. 
28:18: He who becomes surety for a man charged with murder Shall be a fugitive, and unsafe. 
28:19: Instruct your son, and he shall love you; And he shall give honor to your soul, Lest he follow a lawless people. 
28:20: He who walks righteously shall be helped, But he who walks in crooked ways shall be entangled therein. 
28:21: He who works his own land shall be satisfied with bread, But he who pursues leisure shall be filled with poverty. 
28:22: A trustworthy man shall be blessed in many things, But an evil man shall not go unpunished. 
28:23: He who does not respect righteous people is not good; Such a man will sell another for a morsel of bread. 
28:24: An envious man hastens to be rich, But does not know that a merciful man shall have the mastery over him. 
28:25: He who reproves a man’s ways Shall have more grace than he who flatters him. 
28:26: He who casts off his father or mother, and thinks he does not sin, He is the accomplice of an ungodly man. 
28:27: A greedy man judges rashly, But he who trusts in the Lord shall be careful. 
28:28: He who trusts in an arrogant heart, such a man is without discernment, But he who walks in wisdom, such a man shall be saved. 
28:29: He who gives to the poor shall not be in want, But he who turns away his eye from them shall be in much distress. 
28:30: The righteous lament in the places of the ungodly, But in their destruction the righteous shall be increased.
