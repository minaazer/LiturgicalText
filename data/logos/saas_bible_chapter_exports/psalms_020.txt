20:1: For the End; a Psalm by David. 
20:2: O Lord, the king shall be glad in Your power; And in Your salvation he will greatly rejoice. 
20:3: You gave him his soul’s desire, And You did not deprive him of his lips’ request. (Pause) 
20:4: For You anticipated him with blessings of goodness; You placed a crown of precious stone on his head. 
20:5: He asked You for life, and You gave it to him, Length of days unto ages of ages. 
20:6: Great is his glory in Your salvation; Glory and majesty You shall place upon him. 
20:7: For You shall give him blessing unto ages of ages; You shall fill him with the joy of Your presence. 
20:8: For the king hopes in the Lord, And in the mercy of the Most High, he will not be shaken. 
20:9: May Your hand be found on all Your enemies; May Your right hand find all who hate You. 
20:10: You shall make them like a fiery furnace In the time of Your presence; The Lord shall throw them into confusion in His wrath, And the fire shall devour them. 
20:11: You shall destroy their offspring from the earth, And their seed from among the sons of men. 
20:12: For they vented all their evils against You; They reasoned through a counsel they could not establish. 
20:13: For You shall make them turn their back; Among Your remnants, You shall make ready their countenance. 
20:14: Be exalted, O Lord, in Your power; We will sing and praise Your mighty deeds.
