1:1: Blessed is the man, Who walks not in the counsel of the ungodly, Nor stands in the way of sinners, Nor sits in the seat of the troublesome; 
1:2: But his will is in the law of the Lord, And in His law he meditates day and night. 
1:3: He shall be like a tree, Planted by streams of waters, That produces its fruit in its season; And his leaf shall not wither, And whatever he does shall prosper. 
1:4: Not so are the ungodly, not so, But they are like the young grass the wind drives from the face of the earth. 
1:5: Therefore the ungodly shall not rise in the judgment, Nor sinners in the counsel of the righteous; 
1:6: For the Lord knows the way of the righteous, But the way of the ungodly shall perish.
