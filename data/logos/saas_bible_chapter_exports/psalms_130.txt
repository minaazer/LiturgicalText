130:1: An Ode of Ascents, by David. O Lord, My heart is not exalted, Neither are my eyes raised up; Neither am I carried along in great things, Nor in things too marvelous for me. 
130:2: If I were not humble-minded, But exalted my soul, Like a child weaned from his mother, So You would reward my soul. 
130:3: Let Israel hope in the Lord From this present time and unto the ages.
