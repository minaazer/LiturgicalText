19:1: A man’s lack of discernment maltreats his ways, And in his heart he blames God. 
19:2: Wealth adds many friends, But the poor man is even forsaken by an existing friend. 
19:3: A false witness shall not be unpunished, And he who accuses unrighteously shall not escape. 
19:4: Many men serve in the presence of kings, But every evil man becomes a reproach to another man. 
19:5: Every man who hates his poor brother, He shall be far from friendship. 
19:6: Good understanding will draw near those who know it, And a man with discernment will find it. 
19:7: He who does many evil things perfects wickedness, And he who provokes with words, He shall not be saved. 
19:8: He who gains discernment loves himself, And he who guards discernment will find good things. 
19:9: A false witness shall not be unpunished, And he who kindles evil will perish by it. 
19:10: Luxury is not profitable for a man without discernment, And it is not good if a servant begins to rule with arrogance. 
19:11: A merciful man is longsuffering, And his boast goes against the lawless. 
19:12: The anger of a king is like the roar of a lion, But as dew upon the grass, thus is his grace. 
19:13: A son without discernment is a disgrace to his father, And vows paid from the wage of a prostitute are not pure. 
19:14: Fathers divide the house and property to their children, But a wife is suited to her husband by God. 
19:15: Cowardice possesses a weak man, And the soul of the idle man will be hungry. 
19:16: He who guards the commandment keeps his soul, But he who despises his ways shall perish. 
19:17: The one who has mercy on the poor lends to God, And He will repay him according to his gift. 
19:18: Instruct your son, for thus he shall be hopeful, But do not lift up your soul in arrogance. 
19:19: A malicious man shall be punished severely, And if he commits injury, he will also add this to his soul. 
19:20: Hear, my son, the instruction of your father, That you may become wise in your last days. 
19:21: There are many thoughts in the heart of a man, But the counsel of the Lord abides forever. 
19:22: Mercy is fruit to a man, And a poor righteous man is better than a rich liar. 
19:23: The fear of the Lord is unto life for a man, And he shall lodge without fear in places where knowledge is not examined. 
19:24: A man who hides his hands in his bosom unrighteously, He will not even bring them up to his mouth. 
19:25: When a pestilent man is punished, a man without discernment becomes more astute, But if you reprove a discerning man, he will understand perception. 
19:26: He who dishonors his father and pushes away his mother, He will be shamed and reproached. 
19:27: A son who forsakes guarding his father’s instruction, He will think about evil sayings. 
19:28: He who becomes surety for an undiscerning child despises the ordinance, And the mouth of the ungodly swallows judgments. 
19:29: Lashes are prepared for the intemperate, And punishments for the shoulders of those without discernment.
