17:1: “I am perishing, carried away by the wind; And I ask for a burial-place, but cannot obtain it. 
17:2: I am tired of entreating, yet what have I done? 
17:3: Strangers have stolen my possessions. Who is he? Let him join hands with me. 
17:4: For You hid their heart from discernment; Therefore, You will not exalt them. 
17:5: He will declare evils as their portion, But my eyes melt away because of my children. 
17:6: “But You made me a byword among the people, And I have become an object of laughter to them. 
17:7: For my eyes are petrified because of Your wrath, And I am greatly besieged by everyone. 
17:8: Amazement seizes true men because of this, And the righteous man may rise against the lawless man. 
17:9: May the faithful man hold to his way, And may those who have clean hands take courage. 
17:10: “Nevertheless, all of you stick together, And indeed come, for I found no truth in you. 
17:11: My days have passed in groaning, And my heartstrings are broken. 
17:12: Light turned night into day. It is near at the appearance of darkness. 
17:13: If I tarry, Hades is my home, And my bed is spread in darkness. 
17:14: I called upon death to be my father, And decay to be my mother and my sister. 
17:15: Where then is my hope? Or where shall I see my good things? 
17:16: Will they go down with me to Hades? Or shall we go down together in the tomb?”
