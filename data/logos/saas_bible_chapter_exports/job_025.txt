25:1: Then Bildad the Shuhite answered and said: 
25:2: “What beginning or fear is His— He who makes all things in the highest heaven? 
25:3: For may no one imagine there is a respite for pirates. Upon whom will there not be an ambush from Him? 
25:4: How shall a mortal be righteous before the Lord? Or how may he who is born of a woman purify himself? 
25:5: If He orders the moon, then it does not shine, And the stars are not pure in His sight. 
25:6: But alas! Man is putrid, and the son of man a worm.”
