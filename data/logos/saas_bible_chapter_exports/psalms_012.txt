12:1: For the End; a Psalm by David. 
12:2: How long, O Lord, will You forget me to the end? How long will You turn Your face from me? 
12:3: How long will I take counsel in my soul, Having grief in my heart daily? How long will my enemy be exalted over me? 
12:4: Look upon me and hear me, O Lord my God; Enlighten my eyes, lest I sleep in death, 
12:5: Lest my enemy say, “I prevailed against him;” Those who afflict me greatly rejoice, if I am shaken. 
12:6: But I hope in Your mercy; My heart shall greatly rejoice in Your salvation; I will sing to the Lord, who shows kindness to me; I will sing to the name of the Lord Most High.
