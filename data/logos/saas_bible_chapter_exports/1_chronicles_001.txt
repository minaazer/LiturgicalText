1:1: Adam, Seth, Enosh, 
1:2: Cainan, Mahalalel, Jared, 
1:3: Enoch, Methuselah, Lamech, 
1:4: Noah, the sons of Noah: Shem, Ham, and Japheth. 
1:5: The sons of Japheth were Gomer, Magog, Madai, Javan, Tubal, Meshech, and Tiras. 
1:6: The sons of Gomer were Ashkenaz, Diphath, and Togarmah. 
1:7: The sons of Javan were Elishah, Tarshishah, Kittim, and Rodanim. 
1:8: The sons of Ham were Cush, Mizraim, Put, and Canaan. 
1:9: The sons of Cush were Seba, Havilah, Sabta, Raama, and Sabtecha. The sons of Raama were Sheba and Dedan. 
1:10: Cush begot Nimrod; he began to be a mighty hunter on the earth. 
1:11: The sons of Shem were Elam, Asshur, and Arphaxad. 
1:12: Shela, 
1:13: Eber, Pheleg, Ragan, 
1:14: Seruch, Nachor, Tharrha, 
1:15: Abraham. 
1:16: And the sons of Abraham: Isaac and Ishmael. 
1:17: And these are their generations: the firstborn of Ishmael, Nabaeoth, and Kedar, Nabdeel, Massam, 
1:18: Masma, Iduma, Masse, Chondan, Thaeman, 
1:19: Jettur, Naphes, Kedma: these the sons of Ishmael. 
1:20: And the sons of Keturah, Abraham’s concubine: she bore him Zembram, Jexan, Madiam, Madam, Sobac, Soe; and the sons of Jexan: Daedan and Sabai; 
1:21: and the sons of Madiam: Gephar, and Opher, and Enoch, and Abida, and Eldada; all these the sons of Keturah. 
1:22: And Abraham begot Isaac: and the sons of Isaac were Jacob and Esau. 
1:23: The sons of Esau: Eliphaz, and Raguel, and Jeul, and Jeglom, and Core. 
1:24: The sons of Eliphaz: Thaeman, and Omar, Sophar, and Gootham, and Kenez, and Thamna, and Amalek. 
1:25: And the sons of Raguel, Naches, Zare, Some, and Moze. 
1:26: The sons of Seir: Lotan, Sobal, Sebegon, Ana, Deson, Osar, and Disan. 
1:27: And the sons of Lotan: Chorri and Aeman; and the sister of Lotan was Thamna. 
1:28: The sons of Sobal: Alon, Machanath, Taebel, Sophi, and Onan; and the sons of Sebegon: Aeth and Sonan. 
1:29: The sons of Sonan, Daeson; and the sons of Daeson: Emeron, and Asebon, and Jethram, and Charran. 
1:30: And the sons of Hosar: Balaam, and Zucam, and Acan; the sons of Disan: Os and Aran. 
1:31: And these are their kings: Bela the son of Beor, and the name of his city was Dinhabah. 
1:32: When Bela died, Jobab the son of Zerah of Bozrah reigned in his place. 
1:33: When Jobab died, Husham of the land of the Temanites reigned in his place. 
1:34: And when Husham died, Hadad the son of Bedad, who attacked Midian in the field of Moab, reigned in his place. The name of his city was Avith. 
1:35: When Hadad died, Samlah of Masrekah reigned in his place. 
1:36: And when Samlah died, Saul of Rehoboth by the river reigned in his place. 
1:37: When Saul died, Baal-Hanan the son of Achbor reigned in his place. 
1:38: And when Baal-Hanan the son of Achbor died, Hadad the son of Barad reigned in his place; and the name of his city was Phogor. 
1:39: Hadad died also. And the chiefs of Edom were Chief Timnah, Chief Aliah, Chief Jetheth, 
1:40: Chief Aholibamah, Chief Elah, Chief Pinon, 
1:41: Chief Kenaz, Chief Teman, Chief Mibzar, 
1:42: Chief Magdiel, and Chief Iram. These were the chiefs of Edom.
