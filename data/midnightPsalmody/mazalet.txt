Glory to God adored
and His Begotten Son
His Holy Spirit poured
in David’s canopy

Blessing in Siloam
heals every ache and ill
Gift from the Lord to dwell
in David’s canopy

Angel by sovereign word
stirred up the sheep‑pool’s well
Healed all the sick who fell
in David’s canopy

Spirit‑angel came
stirred a radiant spring
Second Adam’s blood
in David’s canopy

Gabriel stirred a spring
then the Lord did come
Healed His suffering flock
in David’s canopy

From the Father’s side
to a garden prayed
Angel appeared to uphold
in David’s canopy

Trod the press of war
won and took the crown
Mighty mends the Fall
in David’s canopy

Lifted up the veil
rolled the stone away
Broke the dividing rail
in David’s canopy

Sowed and preached and taught
Paul declared, He came
From the seed of Abraham.
in David’s canopy

I heard the righteous David
Singing on his strings;
Exulting like the mighty,
in David’s canopy

Jacob dwelt between rivers.
As a secret shared by two
The Church between covenants.
in David’s canopy

I saw a difficult thing
Stone upon the well
Jacob raised it high
in David’s canopy

Rock of sin
Entirely removed
Opened baptism’s gate.
in David’s canopy
///////
He met with trial and grief
tempted by the thief
Stripped Laban’s wealth in brief
in David’s canopy

Bound with love and flame
dwelt in Paddan’s plain
Bridegroom tending lambs again
in David’s canopy

Kept the vow and pact
back to Isaac’s tract
Ship with azure cargo packed
in David’s canopy

Climbed the height of Gilead
with his goods and lads
Feast of renewal glad
in David’s canopy

After him came Moses true
of his seed he grew
Prophet calling God anew
in David’s canopy

Curse of Law a sign
all the rites enshrined
None could read that line
in David’s canopy

Balsam branch was sown
Jacob’s sons made grown
Plucked its spice well‑known
in David’s canopy

Garden in the house of David
vine a cluster gave it
Bread of life, He laid it
in David’s canopy

Palace in the land of Israel
bright with holy rays
Dwelling of Emmanuel
in David’s canopy

Virgin John laid bare
church in length and breadth declared
Spoke and started there
in David’s canopy

Like the Lamb’s apostles came
myriads they claim
Ranks all bowed His name
in David’s canopy

Numbered host arranged
over tribes ordained
Twelve thousand sealed and named
in David’s canopy

City walled and strong
golden, carved in stone
Chosen gems belong
in David’s canopy

Tabernacle Father set
four firm poles were met
Twelve wide gates were set
in David’s canopy

Saw a spotless Lamb
came to ransom Adam
Light for every man
in David’s canopy

Light of heaven’s blaze
psalmist told its praise
Queen beside Him stays
in David’s canopy

Word of this is known
psalmist made it shown
Tyre’s daughters bow to His throne
in David’s canopy

My desire and aim
calling on His Name
Where my feet bend, acclaim
in David’s canopy

Author of this praise
Abba ‘Abd al‑Masih
Offers thanks and lays
in David’s canopy

None to mark my way
rise at dawn to pray
With a thankful heart I say
in David’s canopy

Lord, preserve our patriarch
partner bishop, mark
By their prayers we’re stark
in David’s canopy

Lord, preserve our priests
also all our deacons
All the Church in peace
in David’s canopy
